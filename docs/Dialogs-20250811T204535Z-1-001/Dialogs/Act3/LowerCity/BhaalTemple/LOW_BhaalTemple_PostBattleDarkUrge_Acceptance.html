    Synopsis:

    The Dark Urge accepted Bhaal when he appeared. Now, Jaheira and Minsc challenge and try to take down the DU, if they were encountered and survived until this point in the game.
    If the Jaheira/Minsc scene cannot trigger, there will be fallback comments from the party.

    How to trigger:

    0) Start with Dark Urge Origin.
    1) With the debugbook, go to Act 3 -> Undercity -> Bhaal Temple -> Bhaal Temple Bridge.
    2) Defeat Orin.
    3) Accept Bhaal's demands.
