    Synopsis:

    The Bhaal Temple is closed off by a massive door. They are 3 ways to open the door.
    1) with an Bhaal Amulet, given in the Serial Killer situation
    2) with invitation from Orin - which is given when players killed Gortash, after she has given the players the ultimatum
    3) if players are the Dark Urge, their butler will freely open the doors for the players.

    How to trigger:

    1) Go to Act 3 -> UnderCity -> Bhaal Temple Bridge
    2a) Use oe BTGiveBhaalAmulet to give players Bhaal Amulet.
    or
    2b) Use oe BTSetKilledGortash to set players have killed Gortash so players are invited.
    2c) Players start as Dark Urge origins, and therefore their Butler will be at the door letting them in.
    3) Go to approach and 'speak' to the door.
