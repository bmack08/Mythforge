    Synopsis:

    Triggers when the Orin's stone is
    - picked up by the DarkUrge
    - is first stone to be picked up (as opposed to Gortash's stone picked up first).

    How to trigger:

    1) Start new game with Dark Urge
    2) Go to Act 3 -> Bhaal Temple
    3) Talk to door or oe BTForceOpenDoor
    4) Defeat Orin
    5) Go through all the post battle dialogs.
    6) Depending on choice, will automatically get the netherstone or have to loot Orin's body for the Netherstone Dagger to get the Netherstone.
    7) If her stone is the first one before Gortash, dialog will trigger.
