    Synopsis:

    In the Bhaal Temple, if players are Dark Urge, they have the opportunity to fight Orin one on one. Players are given a warning that if the companions jump in the battle, so does the rest of the temple.

    How to trigger:

    1) First node is triggered when DU has Accepted Bhaal and encounter the Jaheira+Minsc Confrontation. When combat begins with them, Some cultists will join the DU against them.
    2) Second node is when the Dark Urge dies in the duel, this AD plays and rest of combat NPC joins in to kill remaining companions.
    3) Thrid node happens when players broke the Duel.
    4) Fourth node triggers at the beginning of the duel.
