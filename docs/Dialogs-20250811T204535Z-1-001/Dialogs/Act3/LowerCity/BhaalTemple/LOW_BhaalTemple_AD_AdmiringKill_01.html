    Synopsis:

    In Bhaal Temple, Orin would display her kills for other Bhaalist and to honour Bhaal himself with the kills. She kills not just for fun, but she sees murder as an artform.

    If Orin has impersonated 2 person in Wyrm's Crossing (possible targets The Smith, The Journalist, The DyingAttacker, or The Flaming Fist), their bodies will end up in Bhaal Temple.

    2 Bhaalist NPCs will comment and facinate over the way she has killed the victims.

    In the edge-case where Orin might not have impersonated anyone in Wyrm's Crossing, a random citizen's body will be used on display instead.
