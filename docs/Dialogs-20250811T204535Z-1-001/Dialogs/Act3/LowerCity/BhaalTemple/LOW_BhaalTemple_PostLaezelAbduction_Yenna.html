    Synopsis:

    Orin abductions Laezel. In the CFM, Orin-Laezel takes Yenna as hostage. Players could encourage Orin-Laezel to murder Yenna. But this dialog is when players spared her and talked to her after the encounter.

    How to trigger:

    1) Go to Act 3 -> Bhaal Temple.
    2) oe BTEnableAbduction
    3) Open Camp Debug book and add Laezel into camp.
    4) Go to camp or LongRest.
    5) Orin as Laezel with Yenna will appear in a Campfire Moment camp night.
    6) In the dialog with Orin-Laezel, do NOT provoke Orin to kill Yenna.
    7) Yenna survives after Orin leaves.
    8) Talk to Yenna.
