    Synopsis:

    Valeria protests being abducted and chained up during the Baptism of Blood at the Murder Tribunal.

    How to trigger:

    - Load up GustavDev/CTY_Main_A
    - Use "oe MT_Baptism" and then "oe MT_Court" to start the baptism and teleport to the courtroom.
    - Walk to the room on the right - the abattoir - to find Valeria chained up there and saying this AD once you're nearby. She will play an AD every time after the cultists do two chants.
