    Synopsis:

    First dialogue with Hope's spirit that happens after players get to House of Hope.

    Hope is a captive within the House, and has been driven mad by years of psychological and physical torture. Raphael keeps her alive because she astonishes him - and the reason for that is that no matter what he does to her, she holds onto the last of her hope and optimism. That fascinates him.

    In this dialogue, Hope introduces players to the House of Hope. They have arrived here deliberately, knowing that they are not invited, and that it is likely dangerous.

    Hope appears to warn them of that danger, and to help them, and also introduces the deranged vibe of the area.

    Note: When Hope speaks in all-caps, she is showing the manic side of her personality. Louder, more intense, and almost like she's possessed by a different, more deranged version of herself.

    2. Teleport to the Foyer
