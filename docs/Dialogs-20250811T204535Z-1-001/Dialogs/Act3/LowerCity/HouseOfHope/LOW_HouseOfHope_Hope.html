    Synopsis:

    A dialogue with Hope - in person, not with the spirit form.
    First greeting is after players free her - she will start following the player after that as well.
    Later, after defeating Raphael, players can talk to Hope, ask who she is, why she was trapped and how's she connected to Raphael.

    How to trigger:

    1. CTY_Main_A
    2. Free Hope by destroying her chains with Orphic Hammer (or other cheaty means)
    teleport S_LOW_RealHope
