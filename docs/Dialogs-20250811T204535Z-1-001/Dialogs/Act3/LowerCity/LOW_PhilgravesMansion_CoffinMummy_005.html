    Synopsis:

    One liner dialog with a mummy that was in a coffin in Philgrave's Mansion. To be able to talk to them it means the player broke the coffin they were in before being hostile with Mystic Carrion or that after fighting and killing Mystic Carrion they escaped combat and then the zombie beggars took over the mansion, making the mummy non hostile again.

    How to trigger:

    Talk to mummy after destroying coffin in CTY_Main_A 8; 8; -151
