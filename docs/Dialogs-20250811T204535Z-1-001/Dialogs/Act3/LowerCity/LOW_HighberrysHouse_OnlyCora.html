    Synopsis:

    Dialogue between the players and Cora Highberry in the Serial Killer situation. Appears when Roger is dead (before or after Dolor appears). There are the first victims. A flag mark the dialogue happens before Dolor appears and other flag if Dolor is killed.

    How to trigger:

    When making click on Cora Highberry and Roger is dead (by the hand of the player or in the battle with Dolor).
