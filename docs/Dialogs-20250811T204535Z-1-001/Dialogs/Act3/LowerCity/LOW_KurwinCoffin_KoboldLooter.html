    Synopsis:

    Automatically initiated dialogue after players bash/prise open a coffin with Bugthimble, a tetchy and mistrustful Kobold, just trying to live her best life looting the coffin and the nasty adventurers dug her upt! She wears a face covering helmet.

    Players have either dug here expecting to find "Enough valuables to settle matters between us" as the note they found said (Tough letter, found on the Courier by Scratch) or just decided to dig up a grave for fun.

    How to trigger:

    - Find the coffin by either knowing about the note and finding the specific grave, or by going on a looting spree in a graveyard
    - Dig it up and open it for a surprise Bugthimble!
