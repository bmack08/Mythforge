    Synopsis:

    One liner for when Father Carrion before starting combat, with different nodes for stages in the quest of killing him (destroying jar, destroying heart, having Thrumbo killed) as well as reactivity for him dying several times. Carrion is always angry at the player and will always start combat after these lines. He sometimes toys with the player before turning on them

    How to trigger:

    Attack father carrion in CTY_Main_A 1; 8; -160. After long rest, he will trigger this again when starting combat.
