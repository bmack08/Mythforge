    Synopsis:

    Uktar is a bursar for the Guild, Baldur's Gate's foremost organised crime outfit. He is humourless and self-important, nurturing a quiet grudge against Nine-Fingers and her blunt leadership style. Uktar asks the player to retrieve keys to Counting House vaults for him, and can provide a hint about where to find Minsc in return.
    He comments on the individual to whom each key we sell him belongs, which is the only time he shows true animation.
