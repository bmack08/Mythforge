    Synopsis:

    The secret entrance to the Murder Tribunal, the door of a decrepit tombstone shop, will open when the Word of Passage is spoken: Quietus (Latin for silence). The door will then unlock itself.

    How to trigger:

    - Load up GustavDev/CTY_Main_A
    - Go to the Tombstone shop door (goto 68 41 21) (near S_LOW_MurderTribunal_TombstoneShop_Door_ef23ee25-c62e-4fab-badd-4f98e364ebd5)
    - Use "oe MT_Start" to go to the door OR
    use "oe MT_GetDolorItems", read Orin's note and learn the Word of Passage that will open the door.
    - Interact with the door while it is still locked.
