    The player is confronted by some Bhaalist cutlists who've just murdered a civilian in Bloomridge park. The cultists recognise the player as even greater targets - those Orin wants dead. They turn hostile, with more appearing after the dialogue, leaving the player outnumbered.

    How to trigger:

    Player walks in to the area of the murder/ambush at the rear of the park
