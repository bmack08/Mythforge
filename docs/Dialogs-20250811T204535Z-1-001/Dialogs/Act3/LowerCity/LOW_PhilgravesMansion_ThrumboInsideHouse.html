    Synopsis:

    Dialog we can have with Thrumbo once Father Carrion is fully defeated and they have taken over the house. Here Thrumbo will act as a hint giver and help with Oskar's Beloved quest.

    How to trigger:

    Destroying the heart of Father Carrion, killing him and talking to Zombie Beggars outside the house, letting them know it is safe, which will make them go inside the house.
