    CINE ARTIST - PLEASE CONTACT WRITER! (RUAIRI)
    The flow looks complex due to integration of an OM, but is relatively simple after the first stage.
    The first stage is always Nine-Fingers arguing with her underling Uktar, who always leaves before the first player QNs.

    After that, Nine-Fingers introduces herself to the player and can be asked questions about the city. If Jaheira's OM has not triggered yet, that takes priority on any subsequent meetings.
