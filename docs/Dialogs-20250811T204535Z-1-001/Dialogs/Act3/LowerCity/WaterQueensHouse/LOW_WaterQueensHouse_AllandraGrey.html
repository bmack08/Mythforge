    Synopsis:

    Player talks to Allandra Grey, the Flood Tide (High Priestes) that serves the Water Queen.
    She's somewhat cold and strict. Talking to her will give some information about the funeral that is happening in the temple, and eventually she gives out the quest Waveservant's Demise, where player should investigate the murder of her waveservant.

    How to trigger:

    1. Load CTY_Main_A
    2. Talk to Allandra Grey
