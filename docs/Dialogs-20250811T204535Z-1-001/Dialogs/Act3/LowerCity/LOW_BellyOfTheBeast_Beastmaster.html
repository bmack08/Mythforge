    Synopsis:

    The players have a conversation with the 'beastmaster' - an engineer who operates the Submersible. The player can either be sent here by Allandra to kill the beastmaster, or stumble here after following clues from the Steelwatch Foundry and want a way to get to the Iron Throne. They can also stumble upon it first, before getting any clues about it.

    The beastmaster works for Gortash, operating the submersible to transfer people and items to and from the Iron Throne

    The beastmaster can be talked into taking the players to the Iron Throne using the submersible. If he's killed instead, players will have other ways of finding the needed information and/or items to use the submersible on their own.

    The beastmaster should make it clear that he would not step out into the Iron Throne itself.

    How to trigger:

    Talk to the beastmaster located in the Dock Warehouse basement.
