    Synopsis:

    Voss waits in the sewers until you get the hammer. Then he's there with the dragon he rode in Act1 in humanoid form. If you show him the hammer then, he rewards you with a Silver Sword and then leaves to meet you in endgame.

    He will still be in endgame even if you didn't do this step, but you just would have missed on the Silver Sword.

    How to trigger:

    Talk to Voss when he's in the sewers after Sharess Caress. You have to not have signed the pact with Raphael and gotten the quest from Voss to obtain the hammer in another way.
