    Synopsis:

    Dialog file for Ptaris, the Gith born from the egg given to Esther. The player enters on a scene of devestation, Ptaris the only being still alive. Hatched unnaturally from his egg and experimented on in the name of making him 'good', he has developed uncanny psionic powers, and used them to murder everyone who failed to live up to his strict moral code.
