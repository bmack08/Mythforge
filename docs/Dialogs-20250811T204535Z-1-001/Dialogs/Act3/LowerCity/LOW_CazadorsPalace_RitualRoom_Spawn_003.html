    Click on "Attack Button" or choose to attack him in the dialog.

    Cazador will fly to his sarcophagus.
    Click on the sarcophagus.
    Choose "Kill Cazadoz"
    Choose "Break the staff and leave the prisoners in their cells"
    Click on the spawn.
