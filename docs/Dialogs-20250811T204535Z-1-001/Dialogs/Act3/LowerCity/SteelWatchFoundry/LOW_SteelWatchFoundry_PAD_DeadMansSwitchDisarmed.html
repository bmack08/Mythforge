    Synopsis:

    The player has successfully deactivated the 'Motivator' a device that, when triggered, will kill every single Gondian in the foundry (via shock collar) and blow up their families in a far off prison.
    These can trigger the first time you deactivate it (high tension), second time (there's no skill check, but you HAVE to get to it in time), and the third time.

    How to trigger:

    Load up CTY_Main_A
    open Recon and set up Roll amazing
    Go to -1968; -6; 417
    Kill S_LOW_BaneiteOfficer09_0c5259f6-3c86-4e84-b61e-42e9f31691ce
    Click on the Kill Switch that falls on the ground.
