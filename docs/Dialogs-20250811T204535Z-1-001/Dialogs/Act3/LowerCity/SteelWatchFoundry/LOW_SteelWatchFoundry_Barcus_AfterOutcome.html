    Synopsis:

    A completely optional dialog with Barcus after the outcome of the Steel Watch Foundry has concluded. After the other gnomes leave Barcus sticks around until the next long rest. He looks at the burning Foundry and will react to what decisions you have made.

    How to trigger:

    Load up CTY_Main_A
    use the debug book to set the IRN prisoners to your desired state (Use Prisoners saved if you want to save the Gondians)
    Go to -1933; -7; 205
    Use killcombat to defeat the mindflayer and other enemies nearby
    Use any means to destroy the big neural machine.
    Watch escape cinematic, and have your outcome dialog. Based on your decisions Barcus should usually survive the outcome.
    Talk to Barcus after every other gnomes has left.
