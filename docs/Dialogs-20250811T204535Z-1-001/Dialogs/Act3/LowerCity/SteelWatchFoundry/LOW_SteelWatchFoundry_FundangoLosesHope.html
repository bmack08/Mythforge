    Synopsis:

    This dialog covers an edge case: the player has freed Iron throne prisoners, returned to the foundry and recruited Toobin as a follower. On the way to the control centre a kill switch accidentally goes off, killing all the gondians.
    Because of this Toobin is ready to give up. The player may attempt to persuade him to keep going.

    How to trigger:

    Load up CTY_Main_A
    open the debug book and set the prisoners to saved
    Open ReCon and use "oe low_foundry_fundangofollower"
    also set up "Roll horrible"
    teleport to -1982; -1; 420 and pick a fight with nearby enemies. Start killing them until a killswitch drops.
    Attempt to disarm one - because of roll horrilbe it will fail
    observe the consequences
    Finish the fight
    the dialog should auto-start
