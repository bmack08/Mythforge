    Synopsis:

    Outcome dialog between all important characters in the Steel Watch Foundry.
    Barchus may be dead so his participation is optional
    Toobin may also be dead, so his participation is also optional. If he is dead, he is replaced by a backup character representing the Gondians.

    How to trigger:

    Load up CTY_Main_A
    Go to -1935; -7; 206
    Use Debug Book to get runepowder bomb
    (Set quest flags and options > Act 3 Low > 10. Steel Watch Foundry > 5. Get Runepowder Bomb
    Click Neural Switchboard > 3. Carefully place the Rune Bomb Powder)
    wait for the explosion cinematic to finish
    observe the cutscene.
