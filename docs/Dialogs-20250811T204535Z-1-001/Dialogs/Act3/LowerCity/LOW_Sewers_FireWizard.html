    The Fire Wizard interrupts the combat to shout crazy profanities at you. You can take the opportunity to convince him to stop fighting, or you can continue to fight.

    Load up CTY_Main_A
    go to -303; 24; 116
    start going up the path, fighting through the elmentals
    once you are spotted by the wizard, he will start the dialog
