    Synopsis:

    Evil ending where the player determined to take the power of the netherbrain for themselves and betray Orpheus/Emperor/Illithid Karlach. In this case, the player is not dark urge (can be Dark Urge for Patch 6), and not an illithid themselves (handled elsewhere)

    How to trigger:

    loadlevel END_Main
    oe mindbattle
    oe mindbraindies
    choose to control the brain

    (or use debugbookwith Endgame Master Setup)
