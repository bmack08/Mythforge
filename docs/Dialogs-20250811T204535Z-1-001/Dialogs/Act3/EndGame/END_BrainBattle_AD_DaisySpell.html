    Synopsis:

    In the fight against the Netherbrain and the Emperor, the Emperor brings Daisies to fight against players, based on their initial character creation choices. (Non-avatars get generic Daisies.)

    This AD is used by any Daisy as they use their charming spells.

    Drop: Make target drop their weapon.
    Grovel: Set a character prone.
    Flee: Make a character frightened.
    Halt: Cannot move or take actions.
    Hush: Silence.
