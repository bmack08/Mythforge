    Synopsis:

    A companion reacts to entering the Open Hand Temple crypt after learning that Shirra Clarwen, granddaughter of the Mad Monk inside the Sentient Amulet, has passed away and is interred here.

    How to trigger:

    - Load up GustavDev/BGO_Main_A
    - Use "oe SAM_Crypt" and "SetGlobalOsirisFlag WYR_SentientAmulet_Knows_ShirraIsInCrypt"
    OR
    Use "oe SAM_Start", talk to Sister Yannis in the Open Hand Temple after her discussion with Valeria, ask about Shirra Clarwen and then head down to the crypt.
    - Make sure you haven't read the plaque on Shirra's coffin or opened the tomb yet.
