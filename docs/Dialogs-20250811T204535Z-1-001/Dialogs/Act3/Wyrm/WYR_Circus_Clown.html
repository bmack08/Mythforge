    Main dialogue for the Dribbles the Clown, who in reality is a disguised doppelganger serving the Bhaalists. The player may trigger it by using stealth and approaching the stage from behind.

    How to trigger:

    Debug book > Act 3 > Wyrm's Crossing > Circus of the Last Days. Go to the back of the circus to Dribbles' stage and sneak up on him and speak to him.
