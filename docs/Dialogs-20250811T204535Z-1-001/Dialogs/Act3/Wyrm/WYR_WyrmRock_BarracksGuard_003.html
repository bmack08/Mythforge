    Synopsis:

    The player walks into the barracks room and disturbs a Flaming Fist who seems to be breaking into a locked chest. During conversation, the player can challenge him, discover the location of his stash, or attempt to persuade him not to flee, and to protect the city.

    The whole conversation should be in relatively low voice, as he doens't want to wake the others sleeping in the barracks.

    BGO_Main_A goto -5; 44; 199
