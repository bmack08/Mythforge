    Synopsis:

    After a difficult fight and defeating Gortash, Karlach stands over the battle and accepts - for the first time - that permadeath is coming for her.

    How to trigger:

    With Karlach in your party, fight Gortash and defeat him.
    Using the origins debug book:
    1. Load BGO_Main_A
    2. Recruit Karlach
    3. Karlach -> Gortash Confrontation -> Kill Bodyguards
    4. Karlach -> Gortash Confrontation -> Teleport to Gortash"
    5. Start fight and defeat Gortash
