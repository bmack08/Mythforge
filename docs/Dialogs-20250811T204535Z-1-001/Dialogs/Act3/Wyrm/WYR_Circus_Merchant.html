    Synopsis:

    Dialog for the circus kobold merchant, who sells weird magical items and shady stuff. He feels like the shady drug dealer on the corner who is high on his own merchandise. In this dialogue, after the dribbles fight, you can also buy dribble's hand off him (for a ridiculously high price.)

    How to trigger:

    Debug book, act 3, wyrm's crossing, circus of the last days. Go through the checkpoint, and popper is straight ahead and slightly to the right (he's next to stoney and boney, the married couple selling statues). To trigger the options to buy Dribbles' hand, go to the circus stage, 1,1,1, the dialogue, use killcombat, and then go back to popper. You can speak to him and notice he had dribbles hand, or you can even grab it from the chair next to him (which will automatically trigger a dialogue).
