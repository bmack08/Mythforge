    Synopsis:

    Dialog with Yannis, one of the priestess of Ilmater. It seems that with father Lorgan dead she took some of his responsibilities.
    This dialog is available after you participated in a threeway dialog with her and father Lorgan.

    How to trigger:

    BGO_Main_A
    Click on |Yannis| - S_WYR_OpenHand_Yannis - cb5ad2e6-40e9-4377-9669-1c4e4b1106e0 - (-45 45 -6)
    This will trigger a threeway dialog between Yannis, Valerio and the player. Afterwards Valerior leaves and you can click on Yannis again to get this dialog.
