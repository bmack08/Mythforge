    Synopsis:

    Gyldro Angleiron the Smith unknowingstly was knocked unconcious by Orin the Red. He only awakes once Orin the Red has teleported away - leaving the players with the awkward circumstance of being at his smithy. He views the players with suspicion until convinced either-wise.

    How to trigger:

    1) Goto 12.1, -51.6, -51 or bookmark "BGO_Rivington_AngleIronSmithy".
    2) Encounter with Orin who's disguised as the smith.
    3) After the encounter, the real Smith will appear and talk to the players.
