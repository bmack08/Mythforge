    Synopsis:

    Dialog for the wheel of fortune mini-game operated by Akabi - the djinni, destroyer of the worlds. Players can win random magical items with side effects or try to uncover the cheating djinni performs and win a grind prize, upsetting him (which results in you being sent to a jungle to be eaten by dinosaurs).

    How to trigger:

    Debug book, act 3, wyrm's crossing, circus of the last days. Go through the checkpoint, and akabis is straight ahead of you. He's the blue Djinni with the wheel next to him.
