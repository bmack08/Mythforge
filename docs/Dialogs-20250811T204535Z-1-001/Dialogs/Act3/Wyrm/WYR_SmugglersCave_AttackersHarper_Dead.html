    Synopsis:

    The Harper from the The Guild - the attacker group, fell in combat between the attackers forces and the Smugglers from Stone Man's group. Since she died, we give the players another chance to get some information about what happened.

    How to trigger:

    1) Go to Smugglers Cave (or BGO_KidnapperCave bookmark)
    2) Enter the cave.
    3) Choose the side of the Attackers.
    4) Make sure the Harper dies.
    5) Cast Speak with Dead on the Harper.
