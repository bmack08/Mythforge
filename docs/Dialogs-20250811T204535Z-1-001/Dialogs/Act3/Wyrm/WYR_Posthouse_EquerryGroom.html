    Synopsis:

    The player can talk to Marhyah or Dringo to mention they found Scratch.
    Depending on who the player spoke to first, either Dringo or Mat'hyah will be the initial speaker.
    In either case, Mar'hyah will dominate the conversation.
    Mar'hyah wants to reclaim him as property of the couriers' office. The player can convince her to release Scratch, and potentially even to leave the kennels altogether. If they fail to persuade her, Scracth is locked up, and Oyster Boy can get fired.

    How to trigger:

    Teleport to S_WYR_Posthouse_AngryMarhyah and speak with her.
