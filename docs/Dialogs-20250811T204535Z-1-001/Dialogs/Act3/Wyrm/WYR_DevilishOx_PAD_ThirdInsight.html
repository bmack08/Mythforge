    Synopsis:

    The player can once again come across the ox, this time in a small ox barn close to where the refugees stop off in Wrym's Crossing. The player will have to pass an insight check in order to determine this is the same ox they've met before.

    How to trigger:

    There is a small ox barn, close to the refugee camp. Toron will be stood outside of it. The player can open the door to the barn to reveal the ox.
