    Synopsis:

    You convinced the entrance guard in Wyrm Rock Prison to open the entrance door for you. This is the AD she says after she approached the door to unlock it.
    1. If the door is destroyed she is VERY confused, and comments on how it got destroyed during her shift.
    2. If the door is locked she unlocks it and welcomes you in
    3. If the door is unlocked she is confused by that.

    How to trigger:

    goto 51; 12; -746
    click on the sleeping guard to start a dialog
    Intimidate her to let you in
    wait for her to approach the door and play the AD

    if you want to see the confused node: unlock the door before talking to the guard.
