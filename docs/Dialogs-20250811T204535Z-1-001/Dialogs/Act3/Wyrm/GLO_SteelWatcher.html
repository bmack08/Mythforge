    Synopsis:

    Dialog for Steel Watchers. All Steel Watchers in Wyrm's Crossing and Lower City will be using this dialog.
    You are talking to the hivemind behind the iron figure. Convincing one SW that you aren't an enemy of the Absolute will make the rest of SW non-hostile as well.

    How to trigger:

    Commit a crime in front of a Scrying Eye in Goblin Camp / Moonrise Towers.
    In BG approach a Steel Watcher.
