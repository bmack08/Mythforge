    Synopsis:

    Generic. An NPC sees a player shapeshifted into a scary form (wolf, bear, badger, spider, ...) or a scary summon (spider, fire elemenal, ...), and flees from it. The NPC can be a child or adult of any species. They may be alone or with others (some lines plea for help, but are spoken in general as frightened utterances.
    NOTE - Anyone with the DRUID or ACT1_DEN_IDOLTHEFT is exempt from this dialogue.
