    Synopsis:

    In SwA, the owlbear cub speaks in short staccato 'sentences.' He's vicious and feral - but scared. If the player coaxes him with food or is nice to him, he will cautiously consider approaching - but is scared off before he gets too close.

    An owlbear cub - young, but still dangerous - appears in camp. The player has previously spared the cub after killing its mother, and saved it from a cruel game run by the goblins in their camp. The cub is scared, and quick to run or attack - it's an untamed wild animal.

    How to trigger:

    Repro steps:
    1. Start the game
    2. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    3. oe qnight NIGHT_OwlbearCub1_43696ea8-3bff-440d-bee8-9cd912e4e8b4
    4. End the day and go to camp
    5. Go to sleep, the dialog will start
