    Synopsis:

    In this scene, Gale is conjuring an image of Mystra over the palm of his hand, staring at the goddess he’s love with, but who abandoned him. When the player approaches, he dispels the image and he feels embarrassed to have been caught doing what he was doing. The conversation turns to his passion for magic however, and he invites the player to try to cast a spell with him together. The player can do so by passing through various stages of spell casting, and when successful, this can lead to a first player-initiated romantic moment with Gale.
