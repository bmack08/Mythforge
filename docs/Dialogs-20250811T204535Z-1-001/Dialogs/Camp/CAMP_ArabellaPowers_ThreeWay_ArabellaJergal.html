    Synopsis:

    Arabella and Withers interact in camp. You can tell Arabella her parents are dead, leading to Withers calming her down. You learn her power and she will get sent off to Sorcerous SUndires after the end of Act 2

    How to trigger:

    addTag ACT2_SHADOW_CURSE_IMMUNE:b47643e0-583c-4808-b108-f6d3b605b0a9 (ReCon command)
    oe CAMP_JergalToCamp
    goto -149; 16
    talk to Arabella and send her to camp
    goto -182; 17
    speak to corpse tender and discover corpse is Arballe'a parent
    click on both the corpses
    Go to camp and click on Arabella or Jergal.
