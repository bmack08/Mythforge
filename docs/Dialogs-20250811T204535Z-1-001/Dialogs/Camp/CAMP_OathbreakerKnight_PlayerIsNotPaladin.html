    Synopsis:

    When a player from a class other than a paladin tries to speak with the Oathbtreaker Knight he will not want to start a conversation.
    From design: Any non-paladin character talking to the Oathbreaker receives no reply. The Oathbreaker looks up at the character, but shakes their head - they have no interest in you.

    How to trigger:

    1. Start the game as a paladin.
    2. Become an oathbreaker (oe MakeOathbreaker with the paladin selected).
    3. End the day.
    4. End the next day too.
    5. Select a party member who is not a paladin and speak with the knight,
    OR
    1. oe CAMP_ForceOathbreakerKnight
    2. Go to camp.
    3. Select a party member who is not a paladin and speak with the knight,
