    Synopsis:

    Dialogue for Alfira in the brief interval when she can be a party member before the Dark Urge murders her.

    How to trigger:

    Choose Dark Urge in CC. Start game. In Camp debug book, Queue Camp Night, keep scrolling until you get to NIGHT_DarkUrge_MurderOfAlfira. Click on it, close the menu and end the day. Speak to Alfira, and this is the follow up if she stays the night.
