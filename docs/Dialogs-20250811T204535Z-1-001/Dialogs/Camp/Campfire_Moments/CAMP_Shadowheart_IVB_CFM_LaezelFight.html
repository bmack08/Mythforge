    Synopsis:

    (Note 1: this scene can play out from the perspective of Shadowheart, Lae'zel or a third avatar.
    (Note 2: this scene features a time transition of a couple of hours. The first part takes place at camp in the evening before everyone retires. The second part occurs later the same night after everyone seemingly is asleep).
    (Note 3: for much of this scene after the time transition, either Shadowheart or Lae'zel is lying on the ground, being held at knife point by the other)
    A conflict between Shadowheart and Lae'zel comes to a head when Lae'zel learns Shadowheart has a gith artefact, She demands an explanation, but almost certainly doesn't receive one. Later, during the night, one of the two is awoken by the other, who holds them at knifepoint (this could be Shadowheart seeking to get rid of Lae'zel or Lae'zel demanding answers from Shadowheart). Things could turn violent, with one possibly killing the other and other party members possibly getting involved. Or they could bury the hatchet.

    How to trigger:

    - Loadlevel Wld_Main_A
    - Add SH and LZ through debug book (as companions/avatars as needed)
    - Recon: oe qnight NIGHT_ShadowHeart_LaezelFight_a3445b22-7973-416b-b8a3-1856414589ea
    - Go to camp
    - Click on campfire
