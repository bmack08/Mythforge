    Synopsis:

    The player hears an uproar in the camp and think the owlbearâ€™s turned on Scratch. When they investigate, they discover the cub is having a nightmare and the dog is comforting him. The player can talk to them and confirm that there's no danger. Subsequently, the cub will be a permanent feature in the camp, with new behaviours with Scratch, plus a new default dialogue.

    Repro steps:

    2. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    3. oe qnight NIGHT_OwlbearCubDog_902e10f8-e9d8-4eb6-ac0f-9ad8ba8787ed
    4. End the day and go to camp
    5. Go to sleep, the dialog will start
