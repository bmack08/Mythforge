    Synopsis:

    Minthara ambushes a player during the night, standing over them with a knife. This can occur almost immediately after sleeping with her, in which case she regrets what she is about to do, and can be convinced not to go through with it.

    How to trigger:

    Recon (in order):
    1. oe readyattackonden
    2. oe attackondenraidervictory
    3. SetGlobalOsirisFlag DEN_AttackOnDen_Event_GoblinPartyInvitation
    4. Roll amazing
    5. Go to camp
    6. Talk to Minthara. A mindmeld seduction scene will happen. Agree to spend the night with her.
    7. Go to bed
    8. Choose to spend the night with Minthara, and sex scene will happen.
    9. Dialog will trigger after.
