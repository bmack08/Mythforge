    Synopsis:

    Halsin's default dialogue at camp once he's joined you as a follower. You can ask how he's faring in your company, as well as glean some details about your next quest. Halsin is avuncular and scholarly, but his mood grows darker whenever he speaks of Moonrise Towers and the curse that has shrouded it.
    Once Thaniel, the spirit of the land, has been rescued and brought to camp, a new branch will unlock where Halsin can explain that Thaniel's heart is missing, and it needs to be retrieved in order for the Shadow curse to be lifted. Halsin can be recruited as a companion 11from then on.

    How to trigger:

    Speak to Halsin after he's joined you as a camp follower (progress through the Saving the Grove quest in Act One or add Halsin as a follower via camp debug book)
