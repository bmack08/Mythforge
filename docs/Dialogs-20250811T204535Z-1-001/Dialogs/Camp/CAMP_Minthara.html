    Synopsis:

    Minthara, now freed from the influence of the Absolute and from a jail cell at Moonrise Towers, joins the players' party at their Camp. She is shaken from her experiences, and is coming to terms with the fact that she now understands the truth about the Absolute - that it is not a god at all, but something monstrous that lies within Moonrise.

    She wishes to join with the party, knowing they can help her get vengeance against the cult, and knowing that they are her only hope of salvation, thanks to the Prism that they hold, which shuts out the Absolute's voice.
