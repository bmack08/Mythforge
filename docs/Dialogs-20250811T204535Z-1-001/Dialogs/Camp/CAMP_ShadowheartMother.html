    Synopsis:

    Default camp dialog for Shadowheart's mom. She has recovered somewhat from her ordeal in the Shar grotto, but will never truly heal - she's grown old in captivity, and she's prone to confusion at times. Still, she remains glad and supportive.
    She has unique branches for Shadowheart and non-Shadowheart players, as well as branches that occur before and after CAMP_Shadowheart_DaughterTears, in which SH and her parents have their first proper heart to heart.

    How to trigger:

    1. Add Shadowheart's mom to the camp
    2, Talk to her
