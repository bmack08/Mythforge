    Synopsis:

    "Sex" scene with Mizora. The player chooses which of the circles of hell she should draw her passions from, and the final narration puts them together into a bespoke expression of what that combination of passions would feel like.

    How to trigger:

    Ensure Wyll is still in party and negotiate with Mizora in camp for Wyll's soul after meeting her on the bridge in act 3. Several nights later, the option to romance her will appear. Debug book option incoming.
