    Synopsis:

    After the abduction, the player has one final chance to let Yenna stay in camp. If she stays she becomes a soup vendor. If she is told to leave, she will never come back.

    How to trigger:

    Speak with Yenna in camp after the abduction.

    Debug commands -
    - oe YennaPostAbduction_VisitCamp (send Yenna to visit the camp one last time after the abduction)
    - oe YennaPostAbduction_MovedToCamp (set up Yenna in camp after the abduction as if she's already been invited to stay)
