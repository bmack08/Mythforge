    Synopsis:

    Mizora appears and commands Ravengard to fall to his knees, bound by her infernal charms. She then mentions that she will summono some spiders as well and leaves.

    If players somehow cured the Infernal charms from Ravengard, she has an alternative branch where she only summons the spiders.

    How to trigger:

    Triggers if Wyll broke his pact with Mizora and then went to the Iron Throne to save Ravengard. As soon as players free Ravengard and move him out of his cell to the middle of the room, this dialog will play.
