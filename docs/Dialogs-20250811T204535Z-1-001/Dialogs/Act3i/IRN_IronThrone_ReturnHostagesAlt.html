    Synopsis:

    Brief narrator nodes concluding the hostage rescue situation. Should be treated as an edge case. Can be triggered by clicking on 9 different potential hostages and they can be both male and female.

    How to trigger:

    If the main spokesperson is dead after leaving the Iron Throne, but at least one other hostage survived, players can speak to them instead. Can be triggered by clicking on 9 different potential hostages.

    To trigger, play through the IronThrone by using the DebugBook -> Set Quest Flags and Options -> LOW -> Iron Throne -> StartGameplay. Then follow through the situation and save whichever number of hostages you need.
