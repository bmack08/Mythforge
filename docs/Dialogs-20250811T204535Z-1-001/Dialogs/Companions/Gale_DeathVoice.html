    Synopsis:

    The real Gale has died and now magical manifestation of himself appears to deliver a message: he has put in place an elaborate protocol that will turn a blank letter he has in his possession into a Scroll of True Resurrection, which the player can use to bring him back to life. In this scene he describes the protocol and urges the player to repeat the instructions back to him. The manifestation speaks with the voice and personality of Gale, but this is a pre-recorded message (even though the player can interact with the manifestation), which is why the language in the message itself is also more elaborate on purpose. Gale does not shy away from the theatrical from time to time.
