    Synopsis:

    Shadowheart and the player talk after escaping the Nautiloid and arriving on a beach in the middle of nowhere. The player finds Shadowheart unconscious, and can wake her, attempt to loot her, or just leave her. The player can officially recruit SH, who'll be grateful for the player saving her previously. This dialogue only occurs if the player freed SH during the tutorial - otherwise she'll be found at the chapel door a short distance away.

    How to trigger:

    - Load TUT
    - Recon: SetGlobalOsirisFlag GLO_Shadowheart_State_BeachRecruitment
    - Debug book: Go to region - 1.Crash site
    - Find Shadowheart knocked out on the beach at 273, 224
    - Click to talk to her OR Attempt to pickpocket the artifact
