    Synopsis:

    Shadowheart, enters the inner area of the goblin camp - what was clearly once the central hall of a Temple dedicated to Selûne, the rival of Shadowheart's goddess, Shar. She notes that this must have once been a key area for Selûnite power and wonders what happened here. If she discovered earlier clues, she may believe Sharran allies destroyed this place long ago. The player may or may not be aware of Shadowheart's shar worshop at this point, and can attempt to pry the info from her.
