    Synopsis:

    Origin moment for Minsc + Jaheira with Boss Friol.
    After Minsc learns of the Zhentarim plans to overthrow Nine-Fingers, he returns to the Guildhall to confront their leader, Roah. She taunts him with his past evil deeds as the 'Stone Lord' - the player decideds whether to back Minsc and fight Roah, or let Roah's plan come to fruition for the 'greater good'.

    How to trigger:

    Go to Guildhall. (S_LOW_Guildhall_Entrance_a78a0b7b-489a-4743-9e6e-4c88f24cc2ca)

    Swap Roah with Friol by using ReCon:
    oe Guildhall_Swap_ZhentLeader

    Recruit Minsc + optionally jaheira

    Initiate the Zhent takeover of the Guild by using ReCon:
    oe Guildhall_ZhentBetrayal
