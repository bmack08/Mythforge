    Synopsis:

    Karlach reunites with her old friend Fytz in Baldur's gate.

    How to trigger:

    1. Have Avatar Karlach in your party.
    2. Origins debug book -> Karlach -> Act 3b -> An Old Friend -> Teleport to Fytz
    3. Click on Fytz.
    OR
    1. Have Companion Karlach in your party.
    2. Origins debug book -> Karlach -> Act 3b -> An Old Friend -> Teleport to Fytz
    3. Move the entire party but Karlach away from Fytz.
    4. Click on Fytz with Karlach.
