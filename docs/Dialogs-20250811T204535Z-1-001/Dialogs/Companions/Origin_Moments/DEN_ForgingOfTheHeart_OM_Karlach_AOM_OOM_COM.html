    Synopsis:

    Dammon sees Karlach and feels her infernal engine. Him having been a weaponsmith in hell knows how to upgrade it and offers his services if the party brings him some infernal metal.

    How to trigger:

    Have Karlach as an avatar or away from the party and speak with Dammon with her (WLD_Main_A: goto 177; 19; 562).

    COM branch:
    From crashsite:
    in debugbook: add karlach as companion
    in reCon: teleport Dammon
    click on Dammon in the Den

    AOM Branch:
    from crashsite
    in reCon: teleport Dammon
    click on Dammon in Den

    OOM Branch:
    From crashsite:
    in debugbook: add karlach as companion
    in reCon: teleport Dammon
    unlink player character and send them away from karlach
    double click karlachs icon to play as her
    click on Dammon in the Den
