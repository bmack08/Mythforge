    Synopsis:

    Vlaaktih appears as a hologram and Laezel meets her for the first time. Avatar can decide if she accepts her orders to kill the prisoner or refuse. In both cases, the planecaster is activated.If you refuse, Vlaakith orders the inquisitor if alive to kill you if you try to exit the room or theatens to hunt you down if he's already dead. If Laezel is not yet convinced to side with the player, they have a chance to do it here if they refuse.

    How to trigger:

    Go to 1357; 0; -785 in CRE_Main_A. Enter the captain's office, and go thorugh the dialogs as you want. Go to the Security office with Lae'zel in the party.
    Go through the Inquisitor dialog and show the artefact, or kill the inquisitor. This dialog automatically triggers afterwards.
