    Synopsis:

    Jaheira OOM - Jaheira's youngest daughter is practicing with a sword outside the front door - she's demanding, inquisitive and insistent in a way you would imagine a younger Jaheira might have been. This conversation prompts when Jaheira returns for the first time, and I was aiming at this proposing "Baldurs Gate always need saving and so few are capabler" to play into Jaheira's personal dilemma. Very similar to the COM but instead of the player being able to suggest Baldur's Gate can cope without her, the daughter makes her feel integral to it.

    How to trigger:

    - Approach Jaheira's house from the front (near the bookmark)
    - Speak to the little girl infront of the entrance
