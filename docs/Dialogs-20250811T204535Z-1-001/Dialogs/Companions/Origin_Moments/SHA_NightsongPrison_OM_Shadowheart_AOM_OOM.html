    Synopsis:

    Spear AOM/OOM: Shar reveals the final step to Shadowheart. She must kill the Selunite, Nightsong, and rise as Shar's loyal servant.
    No spear AOM: Shar reminds Shadowheart to find the spear. The player (as SH) can choose whether or not to find the spear, and can enter the Shadowfell without the spear
    No spear OOM: Shar reminds Shadowheart to get the spear.

    How to trigger:

    Use the Shadowfell teleporter at SCL_Main_A: -717; -60; -856 with Shadowheart in the party
