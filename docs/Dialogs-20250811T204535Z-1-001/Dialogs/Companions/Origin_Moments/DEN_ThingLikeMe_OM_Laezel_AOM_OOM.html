    Synopsis:

    Note: T|his dialogue has a lot of copies of certain branches for cinematics purposes. Do not try to condense without speaking to writer or cinematics! Lae'zel confronts a tiefling that has seen another githyanki. She tries to prise the information from him with intimidation, perhaps insisting he bow to show proper deference. Key emotions: Lae'zel is arrogant and fearsome; Zorru is afraid she will attack, but tries not to show it - at first.

    How to trigger:

    - Have Laezel in your party
    - Go to the Grove
    - Separate Laezel from the party and use her to talk to Zorru. (Make sure the other party members are far enough away so the dialog only happens between Laezel and Zorru!)
