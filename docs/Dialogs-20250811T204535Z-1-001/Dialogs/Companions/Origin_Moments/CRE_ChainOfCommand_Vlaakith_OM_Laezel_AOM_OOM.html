    Synopsis:

    Laezel meets Vlaaktih as a hologram. Avatar can decide if she accepts her orders to kill the prisoner or refuse. In both cases, the planecaster is activated.If you refuse, Vlaakith orders the inquisitor if alive to kill you if you try to exit the room or theatens to hunt you down if he's already dead. Orphan Laezel automatically accepts Vlaakith's orders.

    How to trigger:

    Trigger the Vlaakith dialog after speaking to the Templar or after killing him with Avatar Laezel or Orphaned Laezel.
