    Mizora contacts party after she's been captured by the cultists. Not a lot of choices here, but you can try and find out how she got caught and express opinion on getting her out. This version if Wyll is not with you.

    Enter act 2 after recruiting Wyll, but without Wyll in active party, and visit camp repeatedly, or enter town area through any approach while carrying a Moonlantern.
