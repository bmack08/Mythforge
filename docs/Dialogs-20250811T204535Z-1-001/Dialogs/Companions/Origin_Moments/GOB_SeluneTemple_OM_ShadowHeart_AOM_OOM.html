    Synopsis:

    Shadowheart pauses and reflects to herself after entering the main chamber of what was once an impressive temple dedicated to the moon goddess, Selune. Being a worshipper of Selune's rival, Shar, Shadowheart is satisfied to see the temple ruined and full of goblins, but also is curious as to what happened. She may suspect that fellow Sharrans were active in the region at some point in the past, and may have been responsible for the temple's desecration.

    How to trigger:

    - Load Wild_Main_A
    - Recruit Shadowheart via debug book
    - Unchain Shadowheart from avatar
    - (As Shadowheart) goto -104, 458
    - Go through Heavy Oak Door to temple ruins

    (To initiate AOM path, make Shadoweart your avatar using debug book before going through door)
