    Synopsis:

    Astarion meets a Gur Hunter that reveals he's hunting a vampire spawn - Astarion himself, although the hunter doesn't realise this. Astarion starts with a mocking tone, but once he realises the hunter's purpose he tries to get some information before leaving or killing the hunter.

    How to trigger:

    Repro steps:
    1. Start the game as Astarion
    2. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    3. SetGlobalOsirisFlag ORI_Astarion_Knows_Vampireness
    4. goto -22.73, 7.68, 241.08. The swamp illusion check will trigger.
    5. Speak to Gandrel.
