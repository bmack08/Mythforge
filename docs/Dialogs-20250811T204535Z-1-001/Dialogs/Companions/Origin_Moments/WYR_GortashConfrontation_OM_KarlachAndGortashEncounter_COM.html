    Synopsis:

    Karlach confronts Gortash after ceremony in WYR.

    In this dialogue, Karlach is facing the man who sold her to the devil. She hasn't talked to him in 10 years and she's furious for his betrayal. The player can allow Karlach to fight him here, but in most cases they won't.

    How to trigger:

    In BGO with Karlach recruited as a companion goto 28; 60; 209 and approach Gortash. After the ceremony, go to where Gortash is back again and speak with him.
