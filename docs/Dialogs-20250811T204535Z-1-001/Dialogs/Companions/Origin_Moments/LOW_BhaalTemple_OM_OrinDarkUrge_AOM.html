    Synopsis:

    Orin, Chosen of Bhaal resides in Bhaal Temple, waiting for the players to arrive. She has given them an ultimatum to kill Gortash and retrieve his Netherstone for her, or the players' abducted companion will be sacrified. If the players didn't have a valid companion for kidnapping, a random citizen will take the place of sacrifice.

    Orin has multiple reactivity to the player's previous actions.
    - was Orin attacked during her Impersonations several times?
    - are players Unholy Assassins?
    - did/didn't players kill Gortash?
    - are players DarkUrge?
    - did Orin kidnap a romance partner, friend or random citizen?
    - did players kill Sarevok?
    - was Orin warned of the players coming and therefore kills the victim immediately?

    Regardless of the various player choices, Orin will eventually begin a fight with the players, determined to prove to Bhaal and herself that she is a worthy Chosen of Bhaal.

    If players had rejected the Dark Urge earlier, she will turn into the Slayer before combat.

    How to trigger:

    0) Start with Dark Urge Origin
    1) With the debugbook, go to Act 3 -> Undercity -> Bhaal Temple -> Bhaal Temple Bridge.
    2) oe BTSetKilledGortash for Gortash killed
    3) oe BTUnholyAssassin for Unholy Assassin title.
    4) oe BTSetKilledSarevok for Sarevok killed.
    5) oe BTSetWarnedOrin for Orin was warned. If you set this, as you approach Orin, she will kill the victim first before you can talk to her.
    6) Go into the Bhaal Temple and trigger conversation with Orin.
