    Synopsis:

    Wyll companion dies if the party proceeded through the door to Ketheric Showdown, but wasn't together with the party at the moment.

    How to trigger:

    - Recruit Wyll as a companion, but send him to camp
    - goto 804.34, -41.79, -26.41 and click on the door, open it
    - you get a warning you may die
    - proceed further in and skip the dialog
    - wyll just dies (this dialog)
