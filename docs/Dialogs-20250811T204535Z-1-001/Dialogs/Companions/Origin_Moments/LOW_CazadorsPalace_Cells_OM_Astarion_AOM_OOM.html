    Synopsis:

    AOM/OOM dialog with the prisoners in Cazador's Palace. Astarion meets Sebastian - a young man he seduced and brought to Cazador - for the first time in 170 years. Astarion was sure Sebastian was dead, but Cazador secretly turned him into a vampire spawn. If you aggrevate Sebastian, he'll be so full of hate and pain that he'll curse you.

    How to trigger:

    For AOM
    Origin Debug Book (Ctrl+Shift+7)
    Astarion - Recruit - As Avatar
    Astarion - Act 3 Origin Moments - Cells

    For COM
    Origin Debug Book (Ctrl+Shift+7)
    Astarion - Recruit - As Companion
    select your starting character
    oe debugbook
    Downgrade to companion
    Astarion - Act 3 Origin Moments - Cells
