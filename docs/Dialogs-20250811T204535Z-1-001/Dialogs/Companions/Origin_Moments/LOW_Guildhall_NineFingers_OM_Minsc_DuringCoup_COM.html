    Synopsis:

    Quick and short origin moment between Minsc and Nine-Fingers (Jaheira optional). The Zhent have just turned on the Guild, and Minsc and player must choose whether to help Nine-Fingers or not.

    How to trigger:

    Free Minsc from the Absolute's thrall in the Abandoned Cistern, then go to the Guildhall. For this dialogue, the player will need to sneak in and speak to Nine-Fingers first somehow.
