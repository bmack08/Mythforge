    Synopsis:

    Laezel wants to talk about the Orphic hammer and how the players must get it in order to free/kill Orpheus.

    How to trigger:

    Becomes available once players learn about the hammer by means other than Orpheus or Voss. Stops being available after long rest or after obtaining the hammer.

    If it becomes unavailable due to long rest, it will be an available question until you get the hammer.
