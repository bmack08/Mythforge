    Synopsis:

    In this dialog the player can make a magma mephit appear that when answered correctly will breathe fire on a letter, turning it into a powerful magical scroll. The language the mephit speaks is called Ignan and can be spoken as the words appear â€“ simply think away the many apostrophes. In the actor context are the approximations of what the mephit is saying so that the tone can be matched to the content.

    How to trigger:

    Repro steps:
    1. Start the game
    2. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    3. recruit Gale
    4. Kill gale
    5. Loot the pouch on his body
    6. Open the pouch with Gale's instruction and take the flute
    7. Play the flute
