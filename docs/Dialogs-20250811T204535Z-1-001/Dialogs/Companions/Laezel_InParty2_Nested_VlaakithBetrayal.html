    Synopsis:

    After you recruit the gith, you can click on it to get the following set of questions. The gith will always be semi-hostile at this point. She comes from a hostile land and has been infected by her mortal enemy; she is not here to make friends.

    How to trigger:

    Repro steps:
    1. Start the game
    2. loadlevel WLD_MAin_A
    3. Recruit Lae'zel
    4. Talk with her.
