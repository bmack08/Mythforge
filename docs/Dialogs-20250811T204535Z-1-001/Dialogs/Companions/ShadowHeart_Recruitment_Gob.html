    Synopsis:

    After Shadowheart arrives at Goblin Camp with the Orpheus box and protects the party from Voice of the Absolute, she asks to be recruited. She is desperate at this point, so either this happens or she will start combat.
    NOTE: this currently only triggers at the goblin camp, but may also be needed as a fallback at the start of Act 2.

    How to trigger:

    - Shadowheart needs to be alive and not recruited
    - Enter Goblin Camp inner area (close to festivities)
    - will play after dialogue "GOB_Orpheus_VoiceOfAbsolute_ShadowheartHasBox"
