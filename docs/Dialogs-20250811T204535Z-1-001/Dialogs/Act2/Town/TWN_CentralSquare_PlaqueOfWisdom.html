    Synopsis:

    Secret room in SCL_Main_A, beneath the Central Square. This statue has a dogma of Shar and asks you whether you accept it. Accepting it require a saving throw, on a failure you get a debuff and guardians appear. If you are a follower of Shar / Shadowheart you get have a special option which gives you auto-success.

    How to trigger:

    SCL_Main_A - (228 0 -847)
    Click on the statue
