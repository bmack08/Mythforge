    The Visage of Obedience is standing near the entrance of the tollhouse. All Visages pretty much ignore the player, they are more like shadow reflections of some aspects of the Tollhouse Collector, you are basically seeing some thoughts/memories.

    How to trigger:

    Once you enter tollhouse, approach the visage on your left.
