    NOTE TO PITSTOP: We have a cinematic previz of this so be sure to ping for that if we haven't already sent it to you.

    This dialog plays after defeating Ketheric once and for all at the Colony Ketheric Showdown. Ketheric delivers a short speech before he dies.
    - If Nightsong's corpse is present in the chamber, she will revive. If she is alive by this point she will comment on finally being rid of Ketheric.

    How to trigger:

    loadlevel SCL_Main_A ; goto 833 -47 -25 (or use debug commands)
    Debug command: "oe KethericShowdown_Start" and then "oe KethericShowdown_Defeat"

    The Chosen Three cinematic will play first and set up some important stuff.
    In order for this dialog to trigger, Ketheric (in his Apostle of Myrkul) form must die during the Colony Ketheric Showdown. This means Nightsong must be either dead or freed from her soul cage, and Ketheric must be past his first stage and reach 0 hitpoints.
