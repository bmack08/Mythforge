    Synopsis:

    One of the memories available in the Colony "Brain Reader" depicts an example memory of a citizen being taken. This memory belongs to a child who was abducted, and should be spoken as if relaying the memories / personality of a toddler.

    How to trigger:

    - Load up GustavDev/SCL_Main_A
    - Use ReCon command "oe MemLib_AllJars" to get all the brain jars and teleport to the Brain Reading Device.
    - In front of you will be a severed head and a receptacle to its left. Insert the appropriate brain jar into the receptacle by interacting with it - the jar is called "Fresh Mind".
    - After inserting the jar, it will appear on top of the receptacle and you can talk to the severed head for the dialogue to start.
