    Repeated ADs from Zevlor, while he is (alive) inside one of the Colony's Tadpoling Centre pods. He is a bit dazed, mumbling about the events that lead up to his capture, mixed with traumatic memories of Elturel, Avernus, and his exile from his paladin order.
