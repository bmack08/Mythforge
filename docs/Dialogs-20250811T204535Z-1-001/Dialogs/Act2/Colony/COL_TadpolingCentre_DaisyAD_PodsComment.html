    Synopsis:

    Daisy AD when inside the Colony Tadpoling Centre. Daisy remarks on the occupants of the pods in the room (it is possible that all innocent occupants died before this point and there are only mind flayers in the pods). The Tadpoling Centre is where prisoners are transformed into mind flayers.

    How to trigger:

    - Load up GustavDev/SCL_Main_A
    - Use the debugbook to go to region -> COL -> Tadpoling Centre
    - Walk towards the centre of the Tadpoling Centre
    - This Daisy AD will play if the pods weren't purged or released.
