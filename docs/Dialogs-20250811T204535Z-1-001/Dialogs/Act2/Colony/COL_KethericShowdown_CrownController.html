    Synopsis:

    This dialog plays after picking up the Crown Controller ring off Kethericâ€™s body. The player will realize that they need to collect the other two Crown Controllers from Orin and Gortash before they can use them to command the Elder Brain. Picking up the Crown Controller allows the party to continue onward to the epilogue/intermezzo of Act 2.

    How to trigger:

    loadlevel SCL_Main_A ; goto 833 -47 -25
    The Chosen Three cinematic will play first and set up some important stuff.
    In order for this dialog to trigger, Ketheric must be defeated and the Crown Controller ring must be picked up from his body.
    Debug command: "oe KethericShowdown_Start" then "oe KethericShowdown_Defeat" then loot the Crown Controller ring off Ketheric's body.
