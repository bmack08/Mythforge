    Synopsis:

    This AD plays during the Ketheric Showdown in the lower Colony, when the player tries to damage Ketheric while he is invulnerable (Nightsong is still in the soul cage). Ketheric will react to the damage attempt. This will play if Ketheric is in his human form.

    How to trigger:

    This AD plays during the Ketheric Showdown in the lower Colony. It will play on Ketheric if he is attacked while he is invulnerable, and he is in his first phase/human form.

    - Load SCL_Main_A
    - Using ReCon command "oe KethericShowdown_Start" to teleport yourself there.
    - Go through the dialog, approach Ketheric, go through that dialog too. Combat will start.
    - Attempt to damage Ketheric, while Nightsong is still caged. This will play once.
