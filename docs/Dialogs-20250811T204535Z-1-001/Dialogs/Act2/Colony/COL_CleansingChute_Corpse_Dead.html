    Synopsis:

    The Speak With Dead dialog for the default fallback corpse that drops from the Cleansing Chute corpse. This corpse only appears when another minor character (presumably one of the tieflings) shouldn't end up dead here for some reason.

    How to trigger:

    Go to the lower floor of the Colony, to the Cleansing Room chute. Press the button on the console to drop the body out of the chute (happens only once). This corpse appearing requires no flags at the moment, but will require some in the future. Give yourself a Speak With Dead spell through ReCon and cast it onto the corpse.
