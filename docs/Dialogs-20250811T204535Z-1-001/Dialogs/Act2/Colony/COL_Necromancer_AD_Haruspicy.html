    Synopsis:

    The necromancer is standing over a bowl of blood and bone, conducting a ritual to see the future. This will trigger in the Necromancer's idle behavior if you entered through the main entrance while he is still alive OR after all zombies have been revived in the post-Audience combat and the players are still in the cages.

    For help with Latin, contact either Tom Ryckeboer or Ilya Voulis.

    How to trigger:

    - Load GustavDev/SCL_Main_A

    Audience Route:
    - Use ReCon command "oe Necromancer_Audience"
    - Stay inside the cages.
    - Wait for the Necromancer's turn in combat, he will send a zombie to light the candles.
    - Wait for the Necromancer's next turn - he will start resurrecting the nearby NPCs and turn them into zombies.
    - Wait until all zombies are resurrected - he will start this chant on his next turn.

    Other side:
    - Use ReCon command "oe Necromancer_Assault"
    - Balthazar will be chanting this when you approach him in the chamber.
