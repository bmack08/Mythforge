    Synopsis:

    This AD plays during the Ketheric Showdown in the lower Colony. After Ketheric, in his Apostle form, calls his Necromite minions to him, they will sacrifice themselves to Ketheric, who will then gain the ability to use "Finger of Death". This ability does a lot of damage on its target, and will at this point in the game likely be a one-hit kill.

    How to trigger:

    This AD plays during the Ketheric Showdown in the lower Colony. After Ketheric, in his Apostle form, calls his Necromite minions to him, they will sacrifice themselves to Ketheric, who will then gain the ability to use "Finger of Death". Ketheric will be sure to use this ability when he gets the chance, provided a valid target is in range.

    - Load SCL_Main_A
    - Using ReCon command "oe KethericShowdown_Start" to teleport yourself there.
    - Go through the dialog, approach Ketheric.
    - It is advisable to enter godmode with ReCon command "god".
    - Use ReCon command "oe_KethericShowdown_Transform" to start the second phase of the fight.
    - Use "oe KethericShowdown_Phase3" to start his third phase.
    - Wait in combat for Ketheric to use this ability. The Necromites must reach him and sacrifice themselves before Ketheric can cast the ability.
