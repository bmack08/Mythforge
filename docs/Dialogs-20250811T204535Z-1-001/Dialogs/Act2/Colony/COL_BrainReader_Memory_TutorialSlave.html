    Synopsis:

    One of the memories available in the Colony "Brain Reader" depicts a kidnapped thrall who knows MF's are in their head. This is one of the brain jars that can be found in the tutorial area of the game.

    How to trigger:

    - Load up GustavDev/SCL_Main_A
    - Use ReCon command "oe MemLib_AllJars" to get all the brain jars and teleport to the Brain Reading Device.
    - In front of you will be a severed head and a receptacle to its left. Insert the appropriate brain jar into the receptacle by interacting with it - the jar is called "Slave Mind".
    - After inserting the jar, it will appear on top of the receptacle and you can talk to the severed head for the dialogue to start.
    - This jar is one of the two jars that can be found in the tutorial level (Nautiloid).
