    Synopsis:

    The player witnesses a bloody corpse rolling out of the chute in the Cleansing Room of the lower Mind Flayer Colony, coming in from the Oubliette. This PAD is their reaction to seeing this, and connecting it to Moonrise above.

    How to trigger:

    In the lower floor of the Mind Flayer Colony, find your way to the Cleansing Room. In here, there is a large chute leading in from the (inaccessible) Oubliette on the other side. Approach the chute and press the illithid console button next to it, and a corpse will come rolling out. A short moment afterwards, this PAD will play as a reaction to this. The corpse will only roll out once, and this PAD will also only play once.
