    Synopsis:

    Hairy Henry, a male bugbear, one of three Skull Lashers in the Colony Barracks, repeated behaviour AD for when he is praying to Myrkul. He is preparing for war in the Absolute's army.

    How to trigger:

    - Load up GustavDev/SCL_Main_A
    - Go to the Colony Barracks using the debugbook
    - Approach Krustine and her Skull Lashers and wait until the blessing ceremony is over (or trespass and tell her you're a True Soul).
    - Hairy Henry will say this AD while he is praying at a shrine to Myrkul in his room in the Barracks. It will be repeated every time he gets to the shrine.
