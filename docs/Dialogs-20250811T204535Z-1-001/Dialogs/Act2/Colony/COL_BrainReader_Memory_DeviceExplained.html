    Synopsis:

    One of the memories available in the Colony "Brain Reader" depicts an example memory, of Balthazar experimenting on a thrall in order to try out the brain reading device. The memory belongs to an enthralled subject.

    How to trigger:

    - Load up GustavDev/SCL_Main_A
    - Use ReCon command "oe MemLib_AllJars" to get all the brain jars and teleport to the Brain Reading Device.
    - In front of you will be a severed head and a receptacle to its left. Insert the appropriate brain jar into the receptacle by interacting with it - the jar is called "Willing Mind".
    - After inserting the jar, it will appear on top of the receptacle and you can talk to the severed head for the dialogue to start.
