    The party interacts with any of the intellect devourers in the Colony, and says this line after the intellect devourers are done speaking. They say this only once.

    Intellect devourer lines are in: COL_GEN_AD_IntellectDevourer_1cde5899-11a9-e82f-a49d-f038a554490e
