    Synopsis:

    This dialog shows Ketheric transforming into his second phase - the Apostle of Myrkul. This can either happen by beating Ketheric in his first phase or by convincing Ketheric of the error in his ways, provided the right conditions are met.

    How to trigger:

    This can be triggered by defeating Ketheric in his first phase (Nightsong must not be inside her soul cage at this point) or convincing Ketheric of his wrong-doings in the COL_KethericShowdown_KethericApostle dialog.

    ReCon commands:
    - oe KethericShowdown_Start (teleport to start and initiate ChosenThree dialog)
    - oe KethericShowdown_AssaultRoute (for the option to convince Ketheric and trigger the transformation from the KethericIntro dialog)
    - oe KethericShowdown_Transform (cue Apostle transformation)
