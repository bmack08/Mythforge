    Default dialogue with Krustine, Mistress of Souls, who has just blessed some Skull Lashers in the Colony Barracks. (Myrkulite)

    How to trigger:

    - Load up GustavDev/SCL_Main_A
    - Go to the Colony Barracks with "goto 728 16 14"
    - Let the Mistress of Souls finish blessing the Skull Lashers. Do not come too close or interfere.
    - Talk to Krustine, Mistress of Souls, after the blessing AD has finished.
    - If you attacked Ketheric on the Moonrise rooftop before coming to Colony, the Myrkulites will be hostile to you on sight.
