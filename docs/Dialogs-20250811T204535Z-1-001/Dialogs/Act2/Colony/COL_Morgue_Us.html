    Synopsis:

    Default dialogue with Us while they are in the Colony. Us is either locked in a cage in the Morgue, trying to sneak away from the Butcher, or thanking you for saving them, after which they will disappear.

    How to trigger:

    - Load up GustavDev/SCL_Main_A
    - "goto 676 21 -74" or "oe COL_Butcher" to get to the Colony Morgue
    - Use "oe morgue_us" to get Us into the cage here.
    - Talk to Us.
