    The party reacts to solving the mind flayer "mind puzzle" in the Colony Vault, near the Necro-Lab. Solving the puzzle unlocks a door where you can find the rewards.

    How to trigger:

    - Load up GustavDev/SCL_Main_A
    - Solve the Colony Vault puzzle. Let's be honest, you're not going to. But if you want to: "goto 737 13 -110" and interact with the mind flayer console.
    - Alternatively, just use "oe COL_Vault_AutoSolve" and the puzzle will solve itself and teleport you into place.
