    Synopsis:

    The necromancer is sending a zombie to light the candles that start an arcane ritual which drains the player's hitpoints and ultimately turns them into a zombie, after the player has extinguished them.

    How to trigger:

    - Load GustavDev/SCL_Main_A
    - Use ReCon command "oe Necromancer_Audience"
    - Wait for the Necromancer's turn in combat, he will send a zombie to light a candle.
    - When that zombie starts running to the candle, kill it with Ctrl+Shift+K.
    - On the Necromancer's next turn, he will send another zombie and play this AD.
