    Synopsis:

    Krustine, Mistress of Souls in the Colony Barracks, sends the Skull Lashers on their way to prepare for the march on Baldur's Gate. Played only once.

    How to trigger:

    - Load up GustavDev/SCL_Main_A
    - Go to the Colony Barracks using the debugbook
    - Approach Krustine and the Skull Lashers - don't get too close or interrupt the blessing, but let it complete.
    - You can also interrupt the blessing and take your way out of it.
    - After this, they will move on into their behaviours - Krustine will send the Skull Lashers on their way with this AD
