    Synopsis:

    A player walked into Balthazar's chambers in the colony, while Balthazar is alive, and was spotted by him (or one of his undead). Balthazar reacts with hostility, but players can convince him that they have been turned into thralls.
    This can only happen if Balthazar is alive at this point and you didn't try the Audience with the Absolute. If you did not send Nightsong to Ketheric, Balthazar would have died in combat in her prison.
    This dialog will also play when interacting with Balthazar in this route.

    How to trigger:

    - Load GustavDev/SCL_Main_A
    - Use ReCon command "oe Necromancer_Assault"
    - Pass through the mind flayer door and speak with Balthazar, the Necromancer.
