    Synopsis:

    This AD plays when Chop spots Us the intellect devourer outside of his cage, without seeing a player nearby to arrest.

    How to trigger:

    - Load up GustavDev/SCL_Main_A
    - Use ReCon command "oe morgue_us" and "oe COL_Butcher", in either order
    - Walk close to Us' cage door
    - Use command "select S_COL_Morgue_Cage_Us" and "unlock" - Us should start following you
    - Use command "select player" and "statusapply GREATER_INVISIBILITY 10"
    - Walk towards the corpse chute to make Us follow you and get spotted outside of its cage, without the player or anyone else in the party being visible
    - Chop will turn hostile on Us and say this AD
