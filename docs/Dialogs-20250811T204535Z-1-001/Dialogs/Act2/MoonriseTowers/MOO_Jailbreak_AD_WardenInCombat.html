    Synopsis:

    Gnomes and tieflings are escaping, but the guards have spotted them and begun combat. These are the warden's lines. She is a powerful and formidable opponent, and is furious with her prisoners for defying her.

    How to trigger:

    Load Act 2 - teleport to wulbren - perusade him to let you help him - through him a tool through the bars (a hammer for instance) - tell him to escape now. Gnomes will bust out the back wall of the prison, if the guards see the empty cell they, plus the warden, will chase them and begin combat. These are the warden's combat ADs.
