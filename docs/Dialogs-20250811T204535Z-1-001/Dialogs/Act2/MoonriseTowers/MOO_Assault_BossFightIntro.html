    Synopsis:

    When you arrive on the rooftop after making Ketheric vulnerable, he will be shivering and furious at the fact that his invulnerability has been taken from him. The Tower is under attack and he's in a desperate state. He will fight the player, unless by a huge stroke of luck the player convinces him to surrender.
    Edge-case path: Audience with the Absolute (captured Nightsong then messed up Z'rell)

    How to trigger:

    * oe unlockrooftop
    * oe mooassault
    Go to the MOO rooftop and speak to Ketheric.
