    Synopsis:

    Dialogue with Z'rell when she's upstairs. This takes place after the execution scene, and involves Z'rell mind-melding to see how players dealt with the goblins, and testing their 'desire' to be one with the Absolute via a second mind-meld. She then gives them the mission to aid Balthazar, giving them the key to his chamber, and instructing them to find evidence of where he can be found.

    How to trigger:

    Speak to Z'rell outside Balthazar's Chamber on the upper floor of Moonrise Towers, after the execution scene has resolved.
