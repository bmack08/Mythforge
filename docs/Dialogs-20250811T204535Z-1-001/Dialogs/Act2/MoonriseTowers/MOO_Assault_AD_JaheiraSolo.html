    Synopsis:

    Jaheira is outside Moonrise Towers, talking to herself contemplating her next steps to assault the tower (if Florrick is available, a different AD plays).

    Once the main floor has been cleared of enemies, she makes comments about the tower and the assault.(Triggers regardless of if Florrick is present).

    How to trigger:

    oe mooassault_jaheiraonly
    oe mooassault_alive 1
    Listen to her on the bridge
    oe mooassault_cleartower
    Go to the centre throne room
    Listend to her idle near the throne
