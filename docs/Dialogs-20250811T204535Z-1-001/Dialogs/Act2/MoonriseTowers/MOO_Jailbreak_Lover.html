    Synopsis:

    Danis is a tiefling from the grove who is being held prisoner in Moonrise towers. In his initial dialogue he terrified and frantic and worried about his partner Bex (who the player may have met in last light inn). He is also a back up to get the escape quest if Lia and Lakrissa (two other tieflings) are dead.

    How to trigger:

    Teleport to act 2 using the debug book, in act 2 setup choose the tiefling path. Then teleport to the moonrise dungeon - the tieflings will be there.
