    Synopsis:

    The player has entered the Moonrise prison during the assault (with some warning against in most cases). This is a final heads up to the player that the skeletons they are fighting will just keep respawning with no reward, so it is best to leave asap.

    How to trigger:

    oe mooassault_alive 1
    oe mooassault_cleartower (kills all the enemies in the tower except roof)
    Go to the prison (right side of tower, stairs going down)
    Kill a few skeletons
