    Synopsis:

    After attacking Ketheric on the Moonrise Rooftop, a tentacle tears through one of the towers and provides a hole that leads down into the mind flayer Colony. The party must follow Ketheric down there to continue the main quest, and this PAD should hint at that.

    How to trigger:

    - Load up GustavDev/SCL_Main_A
    - Use the debugbook -> MOO quest flags and options -> either start the Assault on Moonrise/Audience with the Absolute and attack Ketheric on the rooftop of Moonrise Tower
    - Get Ketheric to leave to the Colony (killcombat or Ctrl+Shift+K Ketheric, keep attacking Ketheric if he's still invulnerable)
    - After the combat is over, a player will say this PAD after a few seconds, while still on the rooftop and not blocked from talking
