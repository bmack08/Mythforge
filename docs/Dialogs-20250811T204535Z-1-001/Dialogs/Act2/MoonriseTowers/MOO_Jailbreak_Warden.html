    The Warden is a devout follower of the Absolute with an iron will, but also a sense of mischief. When the player approaches, she sees it as an opportunity to play with the player a little bit - like a cat with a mouse.

    from tutorial

    teleport S_MOO_PrisonWarden
    Speak to her.
