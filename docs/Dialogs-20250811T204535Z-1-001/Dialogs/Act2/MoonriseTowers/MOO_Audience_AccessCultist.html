    The Mistress of Souls won't let us pass into Ketheric's chapel until we've completed MOO quests. Her tone is vaguely respectful, but not as deferential as a True Soul is used to. When we press her on this, we'll see her contempt - True Souls might be a big deal out in the world, but not here. In truth, she is a Myrkul cultist playing her role in the church of the Absolute, secretly contemptuous of every true believer.
