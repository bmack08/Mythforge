    Synopsis:

    Lakrissa is a tiefling from the grove who is usually quiet flirty and mischievious but has been through hell and back. There is a hint of her joyful nature here but she's much more reserved. She is currently imprisoned in moonrise towers. She usually has one liners, but if lia is dead she delivers the main beats about the tiefling rescue.

    How to trigger:

    Teleport to act 2 using the debug book, in act 2 setup choose the tiefling path. Then teleport to the moonrise dungeon - the tieflings will be there.
