    Synopsis:

    Wulbren has asked the player to get 'tools'. Player has to throw the tool to him in the cell. In this instance, player has thrown something to him he can't use.

    How to trigger:

    Load act 2, teleport to Wulbren (he's in the prison in Moonrise). Speak to wulbren and persuade him you're here to help - he'll ask you to get 'tools'. Throw him something he can't use (like a dagger or shovel) and these will trigger.
