    Synopsis:

    This dialogues occurs when you try to speak to the prisoners in Moonrise and are caught by one of the scrying eyes (who summons a guard who will use this dialogues). It can be triggered by any guard.

    How to trigger:

    Teleport to the jails in moonrise, 571, -629 (at the time of writing) and speak to any of the prisoners as a scrying eye approaches.
