    Synopsis:

    The player turned the Last Justiciar back into a humanoid by killing his rats. The player then proceeded to kill the Last Justiciar and talk to him using Speak With Dead.

    The Last Justiciar witnessed the Orthon attack and has been using his rat form to hide for years

    How to trigger:

    Kill the Last Justiciar then use Speak with Dead on him.

    Last Justiciar can be forced to appear with 'oe SHA_LastJusticiar_SetState_Apperance'
