    Synopsis:

    Raphael is encountered at the beginning of the Shar Temple. He briefs the player about the Orthon - the devil he mentions - either telling them that he personally wants it dead, or that it is between the player and their objective. He then saves them from a trap and warns them about the dangers of the temple, before vanishing.

    !!! Don't remove REALLY_ASTARION tag check from the OOM branch!!!

    How to trigger:

    Origin Debug Book (Ctrl + Shift + 7)
    Astarion - Act 2 - Teleport to Act 2
    Astarion - Act 2 - Finding Raphael - Teleport to Raphael
    Move forward (to the entrance of Shar Temple) and Raphael will start a conversation with you.
