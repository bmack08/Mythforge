    Synopsis:

    This occurs if the player has successfully convinced the orthon to let them stay in his lair. They did so by telling him they'd search the temple for a way of freeing him from his contract.
    If the player resolves the Last Justiciar contract, they can inform Yurgir. If they killed the justiciar, Raphael will appear and negotiate with Yurgir. If the player spared the justiciar, they can confront and fight Yurgir.

    How to trigger:

    Speak to the orthon after convincing him to let you stay.
