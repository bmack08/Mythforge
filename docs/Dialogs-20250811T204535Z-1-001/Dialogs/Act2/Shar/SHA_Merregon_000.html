    Synopsis:

    One of the merregon henchmen accompanying the orthon in his lair in the Shar Temple. This one acts as a trader and beastmaster for the Orthon's displacer beast. The player can trade, learn about the merregon's connection to the displacer via detect thoughts, and convince it to hand over the potions used to control the beast.

    How to trigger:

    Speak to the merregon after peacefully resolving SHA_Orthon_TrespassLair
