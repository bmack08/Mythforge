    Synopsis:

    This Ox is a fiendish creature in an ox's body. The ox will show the player a horrific vision from its mind, and potentially go aggro on the player if the player demands answers from it. The ox could also give a gift if the player is evil, like it.

    How to trigger:

    Act 2 (HAV) -
    Go to HAV region via debug book > select "after checkpoint"
    Walk to the stables or use recon command: teleport -28 30 160
    Then speak to the ox in the stable furthest from the blacksmith.

    Act 3 (WYR) -
    Go to WYR region via debug book
    Either choose the "refugee camp" checkpoint or use recon command: teleport 40 51 -138 to go straight there (the ox barn is next to the big barn at the refugee camp)
    Then enter the ox barn and speak to the ox.
