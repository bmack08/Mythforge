    Synopsis:

    The player walks past the stables outside the inn and can determine this is the same ox they saw in Druid's Grove if they pass an insight check

    How to trigger:

    When entering the Last Light Inn area, walk straight ahead to the stables. The devilish ox is in the first stable, with Toron stood outside it.
