    Synopsis:

    The Harper Inspecter is either looking over a batch of prisoners who've just arrived in Moonrise or has just finished inspecting them and is walking away when the player intercepts him. A gruff and suspicious guard.

    How to trigger:

    -Use Debug Book to teleport to Act 2 -> Haven -> Outside checkpoint.
    - Go through checkpoint dialogue and enter Haven.
    - Walk to dock underneath the inn.
    - Use oe command oe prisonersreturn to spawn Harper and prisoners
    - Go through the arrival dialog. When the dialog ends and the Harper starts to inspect the prisoners, speak to him.
