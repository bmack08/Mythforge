    Synopsis:

    Tumeril is young for a High Elf but passionate about the Harper cause. He is formal, but sincere in his concern for the player, and the fight to free this land from the Darkness. And experienced practioner of magic, he is prepared to lay down his life for the cause if needs be.
    This dialogue covers all his main states in the Last Light Inn. His default state is outside the tavern doors, keeping a lookout. He is a backup for the main quest, and will point the player towards the comatose Art Cullagh, who is pivotal to lifting the curse on the shadowlands.

    How to trigger:

    Backup state (pointing you to art cullagh): Teleport to act 2, after the checkpoint. Speak to Tumeril, who is just outside the entrance to Art Cullagh's room.
