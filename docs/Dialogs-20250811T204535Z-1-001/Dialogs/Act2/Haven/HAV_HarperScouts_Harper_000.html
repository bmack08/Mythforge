    Synopsis:

    Dialogue in Haven for one of the harper scouts you helped in SCL (SCL_HarperScouts_ShadowCreatures). She is mourning Yonas' death and gives some information about the shadow curse.

    How to trigger:

    1. DebugBook > Goto Region: Act 2 > ShadowCurseLands > Mountain Pass Entry.

    2. Goto 57.566; 42.958; 134.421
    3. Agree to help fight the shadow creatures
    4. reCon killCombat
    5. Agree to follow them to Haven
    6. At CheckPoint Cinematic, press choice 1 all the way.
    7. Follow Lassandra all the way to a small makeshift altar
    8. Talk to her to trigger dialogue
