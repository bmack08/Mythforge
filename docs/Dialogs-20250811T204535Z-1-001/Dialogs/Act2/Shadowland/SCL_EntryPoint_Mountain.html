    Synopsis:

    Players enter SCL for the first time through the mountain pass. They reflect upon having had a difficult journey. If a second party member is present, they'll point out that you're not alone.The camera then pans to show a group of Absolute cultists nearby, waiting for the drider to guide them.
    If Minthara has given you the mission to go to SCL the group will be a mix of half-orcs and goblins and the 2nd character will recognise them as the cultists you were looking for.
    If Minthara has NOT given you the mission to go to SCL the group will just be two goblins.

    How to trigger:

    Enter SCL through the mountain pass and walk forward. Will only trigger if you haven't entered SCL before.
