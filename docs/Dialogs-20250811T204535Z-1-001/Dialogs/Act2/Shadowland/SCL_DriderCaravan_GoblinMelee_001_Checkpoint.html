    Synopsis:

    The player approaches a goblin camp. Three goblins and a yena are huddled around a campfire, warily watching the shifting shadows. As you step into the light, one of the goblins steps forward to greet you. You can ask him about the shadowy environs, and he will demonstrate the shadow-curse by killing the hyena.

    How to trigger:

    - Load SCL Main.
    - Enter the main SCL map via the mountain pass and proceed to the goblin camp below.
    - Dialog will start as you approach the goblin at the entrance.
