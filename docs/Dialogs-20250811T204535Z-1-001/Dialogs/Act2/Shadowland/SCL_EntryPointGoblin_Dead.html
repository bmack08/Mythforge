    Synopsis:

    Speak with dead dialog if players leaves the area where the goblin was and comes back. They will find the corpse of the goblin killed by the shadows.

    How to trigger:

    1. Spawn the goblin by starting at the risen road or mountain pass.
    2. Move far from her till she dies then come back and cast SwD.
