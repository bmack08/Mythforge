    Synopsis:

    Harper is waiting for the caravan at the ambush spot. After it the ambush starts and the caravan appears.
    Whispered and urgent - you're about to ambush someone.

    How to trigger:

    In Last Light, agree to help the Harpers ambush the Drider. Then follow them to the ambush site. If All Harpers are alive, this AD will play as the Drider appears.
