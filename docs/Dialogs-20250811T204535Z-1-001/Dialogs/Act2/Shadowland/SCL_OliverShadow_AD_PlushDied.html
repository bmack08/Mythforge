    Synopsis:

    AD that Oliver plays when his Plush is killed by the player during the fight at the plaza in Shadow Cursed Town

    How to trigger:

    1. Use Debug Book - Set Quest Flags - Act 2 - GLO - Lifting the curse - Quest States - Halsin at camp with Spirit
    2. Use Debug Book - Set Quest Flags - Act 2 - GLO - Lifting the curse - Quest States - Oliver Fled to Town - teleports Oliver to the plaza in the Town and readies him for a fight. Also teleports you to the plaza as well, you'll only need to approach Oliver to start combat.
    3. Kill Plush.
