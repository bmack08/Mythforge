    Synopsis:

    The player has received a magical lantern - the Moonlantern - which protects against an evil curse. Hearing a voice from the lantern you examine it and find there's a pixie trapped inside, which gives it its power. The pixie is desperate and begs to be let out, as the lantern is killing it. If you release it, the pixie will grant you immunity from the curse, but only for one day. The pixie has two modes: Cute rhyming fairtyale Disney pixie (a put on) and evil vile little fey creature (true self)

    How to trigger:

    Be anywhere in act2.
    Recon: oe SCL_GiveMoonlantern
