    Synopsis:

    Lassandra after the fight with the Drider, triggers only if players started with the harpers and Lassandra was present at Haven.

    How to trigger:

    - Load SCL_Main
    - oe havchec (if playing in the editor).
    - goto 59; 47; 155
    - Help the harpers and follow them.
    - Go through the checkpoint with Jaheira, Lassandra will move with the other harpers.
    - oe scl_unlockharpersambush
    - Speak with them at the bridge and start the quest.
    - Ambush the caravan, kill cultists and drider.
    - Before taking the lantern speak with Lassandra.
