    Synopsis:

    Occurs if the player clicks on the first mind flayer pod with someone inside. They can attempt to free the captive - Shadowheart - but the magical runes guarding the pod prevent this.The player can leave her stuck, or try to find another way to free her. Via a skillcheck or via Shadowheart herself, they may receive a clue to examine the console next to the pod.

    How to trigger:

    Repro steps:
    1. Start the game
    2. goto -1.66, 17.62, -345.72
    3. Interact with the pod.
