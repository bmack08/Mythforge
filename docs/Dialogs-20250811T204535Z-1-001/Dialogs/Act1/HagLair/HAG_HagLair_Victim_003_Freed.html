    Synopsis:

    This occurs if Lorin survived the fight with the hag. He is dazed and out of it. This is the victim who first say the player as a mind flayer and freaked out.
    1) In scenario one, he was never summoned by the hag
    2) He was summoned and attacked the player and saw the player turn into a mindflayer
    3) He was summoned and attacked the player but never saw the player as a mind flayer

    How to trigger:

    Repro steps:
    1. Start the game
    2. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    3. Talk with Lorin. and skip the dialogue.
    4. SetGlobalOsirisFlag HAG_Hag_State_IsDead
    5. Talk with Lorin again.
