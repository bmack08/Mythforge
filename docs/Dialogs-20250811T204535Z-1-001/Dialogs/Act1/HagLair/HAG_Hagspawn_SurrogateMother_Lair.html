    This occurs after the hag fight. Mayrina is in one of two states. In the top she is horrified, shaken and trying to calm herself. She can't believe she almost made a deal with the hag and explains her deal to the player.

    In the second, she is FURIOUS with the player for ruining her deal. And leaves angry with them (unless you tell her about ethel planning to eat her child.)
