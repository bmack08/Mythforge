    Synopsis:

    Hag mode. Player has failed a wisdom save while wearing the mask and the hag has taken over their mind. Cruel and triumphant. The other origin characters realise what has happened.

    How to trigger:

    - loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    - goto -307; 589
    - Skip through HAG_HagLair_EntranceDouble and Skip through HagLair_TalkingDoor if they play
    - Recruit origin(s) you want to test
    - Pick up a Whispering Mask and put it on
    - HAG_HagLair_PutOnMask will play. You'll then enter combat with your party members and this dialogue will play shortly after.
