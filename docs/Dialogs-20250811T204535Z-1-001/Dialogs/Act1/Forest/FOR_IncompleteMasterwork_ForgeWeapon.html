    Synopsis:

    The player has all the ingredients and fires up the furnace to make their masterwork.This is all narration; narration needed as most of this scene will likely not be visually communicative.

    How to trigger:

    1. Enter "spawnItem UNI_S_DebugBook_31ee023c-d151-40f6-917e-84066772783b" into ReCon to spawn the DebugBook
    2. Use the DebugBook to travel to ACT1: Crash Site
    3. Enter "goto -452.25, 13.51, -381.75" into ReCon to travel into the Blighted Village smithy
    4. Activate the Melting Furnace
    5. Activate the Blacksmith's Bellows
    6. Enter "create QUEST_FOR_SussurBark" into ReCon to spawn the Sussur Tree Bark
    7 Enter "create WPN_Dagger" into ReCon to spawn a dagger in your inventory
    8. Use the Melting Furnace to combine the Sussur Bark with the Melting Furnace
    9. Exit and re-enter the Melting Furnace crafting menu
    10. Add the 'Dagger' to the crafting menu
