    Three ogres are eating remains and arguing about what the remains taste like..Then they spy the player, who needs to prove themselves to keep from being eaten. Two of them are stupid, like most ogres. One of them, Lump, is very smart after accidentally eating a headband of intellect. His voice should be deep, and he should sounds lascivious in the way he speaks of food.
