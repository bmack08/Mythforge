    Synopsis:

    The party comes across a trio of goblins who've captured a gnome and have turned to tormenting him - they've tied him to an windmill and are watching him spin around. Here, the player approaches while using wildshape (they're in animal form, so the scene unfolds in a shortened manner that quickly turns to combat).

    How to trigger:

    Repro steps:
    1. Start the game with a druid and level up (Ctrl-shift-L on the character)
    2. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    3. Debug Book
    Goto region > Act 1 > Forest > Abandoned Village
    4. Kill combat
    5. Head towards the windmill up the stairs.
    6. Morph into any wildshape.
    7. Talk to Fezzerk (Goblin with great axe)
