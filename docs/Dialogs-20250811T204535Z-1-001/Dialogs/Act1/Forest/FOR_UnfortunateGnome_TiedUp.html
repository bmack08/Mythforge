    Synopsis:

    The player has stopped the windmill from rotating and can now cut Barcus Wroot, the captive gnome, free from its sails. They can do this immediately, or else demand payment first, much to the gnome's frustration.

    How to trigger:

    - Go to 18 416
    - Get through the goblins by playing as a drow and forcing them to leave
    - Go to the back of the windmill (-11 428) and flick the release brake lever
    - Talk to the gnome
