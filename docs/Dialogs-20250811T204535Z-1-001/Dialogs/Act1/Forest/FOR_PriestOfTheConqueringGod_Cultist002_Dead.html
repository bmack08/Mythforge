    Synopsis:

    Novice Brynna, a junior member of the cult of the Absolute, is dead. Here, the player casts a spell to grant limited and temporary resurrection for the purpose of questioning her. From her, they can learn more about the cult's hierarchy, specfically the meaning of the 'True Soul' rank that is frequently referred to. Her ability to communicate after death is limited - cryptic, broken or repetitive answers to a small range of questions. If she recognises her killer, she can refuse to answer or else lie.
