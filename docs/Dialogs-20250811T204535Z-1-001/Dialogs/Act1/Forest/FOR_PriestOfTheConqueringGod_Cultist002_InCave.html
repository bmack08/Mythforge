    Synopsis:

    Novice Brynna. a junior member of the cult of the Absolute, has been sent by hersupposed superior to attack an owlbear - a wild creature that killed another cultist. She reacts to the monster's presence before attacking it, then seeks orders from the player once it is dead.

    How to trigger:

    These are pretty simple dialogues that occur in the cave after you fight the owlbear. You need to goto 141, 444, convince the cultists they need to kill the owl bear, then go down with them to the owl bear cave, which is at 83, 447. Fight with the Owlbear, kill it and the cultist must survive (kill one or the other to test different path)
