    Synopsis:

    If Halsin is killed in bear form in the Wolf Pens and the party knew about Halsin, the companions comment on the fact that the large dead elf who can turn in to a bear was probably the druid they were sent to find. Oops.

    How to trigger:

    Go to the Wolf Pens. Get Halsin killed or kill him yourself. At the end of the combat the dialog will automatically trigger.
