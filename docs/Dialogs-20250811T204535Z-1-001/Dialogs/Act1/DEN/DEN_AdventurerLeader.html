    Synopsis:

    Adventurer leader dialogue. Mostly used during Adventurers Quest. This is after the player has stopped Zevlor and Aradin from fighting one another. Aradin returns to his den to lick his wounds.

    How to trigger:

    Repro steps:
    1. Start the game.
    2. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    3. Trigger attack on Den and resolve it (goto 210.35, 26.58, 402.82, advance on the Den and use killcombat)
    4. Trigger Aradin and Zevlor situation and resolve it without a fight (goto 205.71, 31.09, 480.66 and use Shift-Q on persuasion check).
    5. goto 166.38, 19.85, 543.64
    6. Talk with Aradin.
