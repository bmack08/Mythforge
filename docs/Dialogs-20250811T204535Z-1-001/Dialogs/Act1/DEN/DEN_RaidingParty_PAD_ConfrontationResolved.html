    VB that plays after DEN_RaidingParty_Confrontation. The dialog ends either with peace having been achieved or with one leader being knocked out by either the opposing leader or a player.

    This VB has a greeting for each outcome, but there really are three main categories: Player punched a leader, the leaders punched each other OR piece was achieved.

    Requires two speakers.

    Speaker 1 is the player who was in the Confrontation dialog, and as such may have punched one of the leaders.

    Speaker 2 is an accompanying character and as such may be opinionated.
