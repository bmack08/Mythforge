    Synopsis:

    Nettie is the apprentice of Halsin, the missing druid. We can tell her about the parasite in our heads. She listens with compassion but decides the player is too dangerous and must be quarantined. If she knows about the parasite, she'll invite the player to come with her deeper into her rooms for "treatement."

    How to trigger:

    loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    goto -438.65, 1.46, -7.12
