    Synopsis:

    This dialogue can occur in three different states:
    In the first, Aelar is in a trance, chanting a spell that will cover the druid grove in thorns.
    In the second, the ritual has been stopped after a druid named Kagha was exposed as a Shadow Druid. Aelar is in a daze - several days of chanting have left him a bit loopy.
    In the third, a group of goblins that had threatened the grove has been dealt with and the grove is considered safe again. In this state, Aelar is still exhausted, but he's gathered his senses, more or less.

    The player always encounters Aelar around a sacred druid pool.
