    Synopsis:

    Not critpath. Covers the grieving tiefling pair at the gate and their faceoff with the Captive Goblin. At the start of N1196, Arka is aiming a crossbow at Sazza the goblin. The player can step into the path of the shot and convince her not to kill the goblin.

    How to trigger:

    Repro steps:
    1. Start the game
    2. loadlevel Wild_Main_A
    3. Goto 209, 429 to trigger Plea At The Gates
    4. Skip through dialogue
    5. Killcombat to finish the combat
    6. Find Kanon's body on top of the gate
    7. Talk to Arka or Memnos, who are standing over him
