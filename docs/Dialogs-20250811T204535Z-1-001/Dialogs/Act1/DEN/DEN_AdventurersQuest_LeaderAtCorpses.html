    Synopsis:

    Aradin comes across the corpses of some of his friends, who were lost during the goblin raid. He's in a neutral location at a bridge and observing the corpses of his people and of goblins, who were also killed in the fight.

    How to trigger:

    OE code to setup dialogue:
    oe denraiddonenopeace

    Without OE code:
    Go to Den and trigger 'plea at gates'... after this, Aradin will get into an argument with Zevlor and needs to leave. He can then be found near dead bodies beside bridge near goblin camp. Co-ordinates X:86, Y:410
