    Zevlor is triumphant and emotional after his people stood against impossible odds. If you know he's a follower of the cult, he'll change his tone slightly when turning to address you.

    Talk to Zevlor and then blow the horn.
    Side with the Tieflings.
    ReCon: killcombat until all of the goblins are dead.
    Talk to Zevlor, then he will approach the ladder and begin the speech.
