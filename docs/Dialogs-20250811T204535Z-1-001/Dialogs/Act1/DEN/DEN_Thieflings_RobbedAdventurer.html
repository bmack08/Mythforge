    Synopsis:

    Non-critpath. A human adventurer accuses a tiefling child of stealing a necklace from him. A tiefling guard may intervene if things get out of hand. Barth: Angry, but gruff and restrained. Meli: Fearless, urging Barth on.

    How to trigger:

    Repro steps:
    1. Strat the game
    2. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    3. oe denraiddone
    4. oe rk_start
    that will bring you straight to him and the kid
    Notes: if you need the path where you have the locket you can set "oe rk_getlocket"
