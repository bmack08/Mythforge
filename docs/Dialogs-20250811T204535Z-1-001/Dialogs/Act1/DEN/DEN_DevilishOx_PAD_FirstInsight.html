    Synopsis:

    The player walks past and, if they notice (pass an insight check), responds to the ox looking at them strangely.

    How to trigger:

    Walk to the tiefling cave, past Rolan and to the right. There will be two ox and Toron standing closeby. When close enough, a passive insight check will trigger.
