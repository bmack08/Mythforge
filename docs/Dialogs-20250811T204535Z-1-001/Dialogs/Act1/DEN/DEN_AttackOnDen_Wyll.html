    Synopsis:

    Wyll dialogue if he is an NPC and is in the goblin camp after attack on den victory. In paths where the attack has yet to begin, Wyll is enthusiastic for you to blow the horn and call for battle. After battle, you can recruit Wyll.

    How to trigger:

    oe readyattackonden
    SetGlobalOsirisFlag DEN_AttackOnDen_State_DenVictory
    teleport S_Player_Wyll

    SetGlobalOsirisFlag DEN_AttackOnDen_State_DenVictory
    SetGlobalOsirisFlag Wyll_Recruitment_Event_RecruitedOnce
    teleport S_Player_Wyll
