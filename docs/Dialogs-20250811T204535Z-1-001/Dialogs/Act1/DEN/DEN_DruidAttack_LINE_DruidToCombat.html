    Synopsis:

    The player angered the druids by tampering with the Idol of Silvanus of by performing a serious crime.
    Kagha reacts if it's triggering near her. Otherwise, 'generic' druids are used for the off screen voices and call to arms shot.
    Can also trigger if the player goes hostile to Kagha in dialogue.

    How to trigger:

    Steal the Idol of Silvanus or anger a druid with a serious crime.
    If a combat already starter, this plays on the player's turns
    All players in the druids and tieflings area are added to this transition dialog
