    This is for the edgecase in which one of the parents has died. In this case - Locke, the husband survives. In the first scenarion he rewards and thanks the player for saving his daughter's life - lamenting briefly on their lives without Arabella's mother.

    In the second, he learns that Arabella has died. He is overcome with grief and rage at losing both his wife and daughter.
