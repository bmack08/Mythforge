    Synopsis:

    Pandirna is guard who is either 1) sprawled on the ground, unable to move. 2) healed due to the player's help. If she's healed, she is ready for a fight and eager to prove herself and protect those she loved. If she paralyzed, she just wants the player to protect the other tieflings - she's convinced she'll die. If she survives, she thanks the player - knowing it is directly because of them that she survived.
