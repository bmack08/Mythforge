    Synopsis:

    Five druids participate in a ritual around a sacred pool. The chant will summon a coccoon of thorns that will encircle their entire grove. Lines play sequentially, each druid saying one line, then the next, etc.

    They're chanting at a normal to low volume. They're attempting to suss power out from the earth itself, but they're not forcing it out. There should be a loose rhythm to it (think Arwen from LoTR when she summons the river horses), but it shouldn't come across like a drum circle.
