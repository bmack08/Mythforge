    Synopsis:

    After the main confrontation dialogue, one leader may attack the other. This dialogue the plays if the Adventurer Leader is the sole survivor regardless of who attacked who.

    How to trigger:

    Repro Steps:
    1. Start the game.
    2. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    3. goto 212.35, 26.28, 400.38
    4. Advance towards the gate to trigger Attack on Den cinematic.
    5. killcombat
    6. Advance into the Grove.
    7. KO or let Zevlor KO Aradin.
    8. Talk with Aradin
