    Small dialogue when you find Liam sleeping in the den after freeing him in the goblin camp.

    How to trigger:

    - goto -73, 16, 356
    - Use your Illithid powers on the goblin to gain entry
    - goto 333, 41, -34, pass Charisma Check with Spike to make him leave

    - Talk to Liam and have him return to the Druid's Den
    - Trigger and complete 'Plea at the Gate' at (217, 32, 411) using the ReCon command "killcombat"
    - Speak with Liam at (171, 20, 547)
