    Synopsis:

    Zevlor or Asharak are manning the wall of the grove, which is sealed up tight against a coming invasion. They're astonished to see an ally (the player) emerge from the trees and order their soldiers not to shoot.

    How to trigger:

    for zevlor path
    from crashsite:
    oe readyattackonden
    walk up to the front of the den gate
    dialogue triggers automatically

    for asharak path:
    same as above but kill zevlor before triggering (ctrl shift k)
    asharak will stand in then
