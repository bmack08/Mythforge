    Synopsis:

    This dialogue can occur in three different states:
    In the first, Inwe is in a trance, chanting a spell that will cover the druid grove in thorns.
    In the second, the ritual has been stopped after a druid named Kagha was exposed as a Shadow Druid. Inwe is ashamed at her involvement.
    In the third, a group of goblins that had threatened the grove has been dealt with and the grove is considered safe again. In this state, Inwe is still a bit shaken - she knows the threat is over, but she's still jumping at shadows.

    The player always encounters Inwe around a sacred druid pool.
