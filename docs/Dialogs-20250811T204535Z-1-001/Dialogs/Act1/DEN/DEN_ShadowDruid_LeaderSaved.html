    Synopsis:

    Kagha's dialog after the Celebration (post-AttackOnDen or post-GoblinHunt), if the scene with the parents occured and the player saved her.
    CAMP_GoblinHuntCelebration_Event_SavedKagha = true

    How to trigger:

    goto 205 406
    oe denraiddone
    walk inside the grove to the middle of the cave
    oe snakecourtdone
    oe killgobleaders
    goto 211 456
    talk to Zevlor (he's up the ladder there)
    go to camp, after the camp intro you can see that a bunch of tieflings are there
    goto -629 -161 (that's a position in the camp, where Kagha + the parents are)
    If parents aren't visible (previous bug) use:
    setOnstage S_DEN_ParentA
    setOnstage S_DEN_ParentB
    Talk to one of the parents. Use shift+Q or roll amazing to make sure you succeed your persuasion and save Kagha. You can skip through the dialog
    Now Kagha has the dialog DEN_ShadowDruid_LeaderSaved
