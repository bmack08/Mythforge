    Synopsis:

    Auntie Ethel has been cornered by two brothers - Demir and Johl - who claim Ethel is a hag and has taken their sister. Ethel is pleading that she has no idea what they're talking about and begs the player to help her.
    Ethel is actually a hag. Should we step back and do thing, she summons monsters to cut the two brothers down. Should we side with her, we have to kill the brothers. Should we side with the brothers, Ethel is angry and disappears with a threat.
