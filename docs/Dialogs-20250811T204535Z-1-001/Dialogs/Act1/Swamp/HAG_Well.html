    Synopsis:

    This well sits just outside of the hag's teahouse. When the player drinks from it, they feel a rush of power and gain a positive status (regardless of whether the illusion is up or down). However, once they enter combat with the hag, it turns to poison in their veins.

    How to trigger:

    From crash site:
    teleport S_HAG_Well
    click on well
    oe hagblight for no illusion path
