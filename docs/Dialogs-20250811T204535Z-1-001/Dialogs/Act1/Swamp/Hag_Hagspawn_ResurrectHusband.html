    Synopsis:

    The player has discovered a wand that will allow them to raise Mayrina's husband from the dead. Mayrina is standing by the coffin that holds her dead husband. When player raises Connor from the dead, the coffin door bursts open and he crawls out of it a zombie - unable to speak in anything other than moans and groans. Player can take the zombie as a summon, kill the zombie, or give the zombie to Mayrina.

    How to trigger:

    - loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    - oe HAG_SetupResurrection
