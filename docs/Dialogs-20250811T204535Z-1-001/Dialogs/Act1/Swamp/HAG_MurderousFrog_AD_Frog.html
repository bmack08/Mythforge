    Synopsis:

    This is a frog in the hag's swamp. When the hag is not defeated she is fighting the hag's control - hissing and ribbiting in pain as she struggles not to hurt anyone.
    When the hag is defeated, she is cheerfully ribbiting as she hops about the swamp.
    In the top two nodes, she is showing the player a stash of loot as a reward for freeing her from the hag. Only available with SwA.
