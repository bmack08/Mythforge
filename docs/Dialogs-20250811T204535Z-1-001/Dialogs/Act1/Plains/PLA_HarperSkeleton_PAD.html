    Synopsis:

    This is a booster for PLA. The player comes across a pair of old skeletons with harper and druid equipment. A successful history skill check will reveal these two were probably part of the Harper/druid alliance that fought against Ketheric's army of Shar. If the player has seen the frescos in the Druid's Circle of the Den depicting the battle between the Sharran troops and the Harpers/Druids, they will automatically jump to the 'Success' node.
