    Synopsis:

    In situations where Rugan survives after being beaten and tortured by the Zhentarim, players can interact with him in the chair he's tied to. He's exhausted, badly beaten, and barely capable of speaking full sentences.

    How to trigger:

    - loadlevel WLD_Main;
    - goto 31.06, 44.73, 602.95;
    - kill gnolls;
    - talk to Rugan, take the cargo (through persuasion);
    - goto 273.49, 5.11, -273.83;
    - start combat with Zhents and use killcombat right after combat starts;
    - talk to Rugan
