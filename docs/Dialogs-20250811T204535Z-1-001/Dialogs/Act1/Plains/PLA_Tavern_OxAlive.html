    Synopsis:

    An agitated ox, trapped in a stable near a burning inn. The inn was attacked by goblins and drow in the night. Players may try to calm him - if they fail, he attack.
    In his SwA version, he speaks like a human. He is initially in a state of terrified rage, barely able to form sentences. If he's pacified, he is calm and placid.
    The ox is standing in a stall, a dead goblin at his feet. When players arrive, he is agitated, ready to attack at a moment's notice. If calmed, he will relax and moo peacefully.
