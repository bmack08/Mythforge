    Synopsis:

    An artist captued by the Zhentarim, Oskar is being forced to paint against his will. He'll discreetly approach the player and beg them to buy him, mindful that he's within earshot of his brutal captors. If he is freed by whatever means, he'll regain some of his old haughtiness but will ultimately still be grateful as he relates his story - that he's torn between two loves. The first is the patron who has offered to marry him, and the second is a first true love from his pre-fame and -wealth past.
