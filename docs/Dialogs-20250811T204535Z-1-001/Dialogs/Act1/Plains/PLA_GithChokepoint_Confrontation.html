    Synopsis:

    Our party encounters a band of githyanki warriors who have taken position at bottleneck in the region - a bridge that provides one of the few paths of progression. The gith confront us and try to see if we know anything about the 'weapon' they seek. (The weapon in question is the strange puzzle box we are carrying.) While reading our thoughts, the githyanki may realise we are tadpoled, resulting in instant combat. If we manage to conceal our tadpoles, they may allow us to pass.
