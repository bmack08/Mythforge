    Synopsis:

    The Cult of the Absolute has ransacked the inn at Waukeen's Rest and kidnapped Duke Stelmane of Baldur's Gate. The player arrives after the carnage, finding corpses strewn about. The player casts 'Speak with Dead' on one of these corpses - a member of the Flaming Fist who was sworn to protect the Duke. She was among the final Fists left standing. She was killed guarding the Duke.
