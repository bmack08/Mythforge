    Synopsis:

    One of the goblins is torturing a prisoner, either to find out where the grove is, or just because he wants to. The player can try to get rid of him - if Shadowheart is present, she gives some advice on how to do so. If Shadowheart is the avatar, she has unique options criticising the goblin's techniques.

    How to trigger:

    from crashsite:
    goto 318.94, 40.81, -19.95
    walk towards liam on torture rack
    dialogue triggers automatically
