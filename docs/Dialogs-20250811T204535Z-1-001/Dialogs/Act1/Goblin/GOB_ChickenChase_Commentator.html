    Synopsis:

    This goblin is a commentator on the much-loved goblin sport of chicken-chasing, which involves chasing a chicken (or later, a much larger owlbear) through a set of posts to score a 'chicken-strike'. She's fanatically devoted to the sport, and expects the same dedication from everyone. She'll invite the player to take part, but only if you make a bet.
