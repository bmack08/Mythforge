    Synopsis:

    A goblin comes up to you after you've poisoned the beer and gives you a glass of newly poisoned alcohol, proposing a toast. She's standing on a cage (about 2m high) together with the player.

    How to trigger:

    - In DebugBook: GoToRegion -> Act1 -> GOB - GoblinCamp -> Teleport to Festivities Area (anything)
    - In DebugBook: Set quest flags & option -> Act1 -> GOB - Goblin Camp -> Goblin Toast -> Poison the beer tub
    - Get near the goblin stage
