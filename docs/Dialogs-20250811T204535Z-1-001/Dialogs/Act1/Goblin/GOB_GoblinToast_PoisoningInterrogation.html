    Synopsis:

    One of the goblins stops the player and accusses them of poisoning their beer and thus killing some of the goblins.

    How to trigger:

    - loadmodule Gustav WLD_Main_A
    - god
    - roll amazing
    - create OBJ_BasicPoison
    - goto -94.25, 9.78, 442.25
    - Use the Booze Tub nearby.
    - Combine Basic Poison with Booze Tub.
    - You will be spotted. Choose dialogue option: 2 > 1 > 3 > 2
    - Wait until some of the goblins die to poison.
    - GOB_GoblinToast_PoisoningInterrogation will trigger after one goblin approach you.
