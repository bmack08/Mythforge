    Synopsis:

    Before entering the goblin camp, a scene triggers in which the party hear the voice of the Absolute, and experience a vision of its Chosen Three (Ketheric, Gortash and Orin). The voice orders the player to help the three find the Orpheus artefact and to seek 'the path of illumination'.
    If a party member in the group has the Orpheus box in their inventory, they take it into their hands. It glows brighter and brighter, and then the voice and visions stop. The light from the box then fades, and the player(s) discuss the apparent protection the box provided from the Absoluteâ€™s influence.
    If no party member in the group has the Orpheus box in their inventory,and Shadowheart isn't alive to show up, the visions and voice will cause the party to pass out and experience a forced camp night where they receive the box.
    NOTE: this dialogue contains narrator nodes that're intended to be player-forward in the medium term until NPC assets are available for cinematics.

    How to trigger:

    Enter the goblin camp across stone bridge OR:

    Teleport to one of the following location (7 zone trigger)
