    Synopsis:

    If the player released the captured goblin in Halsin's den, the CG will be waiting for the player at the checkpoint. When the player starts the dialog with the main guard at the checkpoint (or gets closer to him) and the CG is at the checkpoint, this dialog is shown. After this dialog, the CG leaves the checkpoint and the main guard gets his normal dialog back. Olak and Sazza have a very antagonistic relationship, ripping each other to shreds.

    How to trigger:

    go to region - act 1 - goblin camp - start
    debugbook - quest flags - act 1 - goblin camp - checkpoint - call capturedgoblin
    sazza will run across bridge to checkpoint, follow her and dialog triggers automatically
