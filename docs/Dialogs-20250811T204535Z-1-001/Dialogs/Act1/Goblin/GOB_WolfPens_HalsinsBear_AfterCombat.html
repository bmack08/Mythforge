    Synopsis:

    Halsin, arch-druid of the Emerald Grove, is freed from the goblins and changes back from bear form, thanking the players. They can ask him about their tadpole problem, but he wants help saving the grove first - either by killing the goblin leadership or defending the grove, whichever is applicable. The player can insist on learning what they can from him to help with their tadpole, if they wish.
