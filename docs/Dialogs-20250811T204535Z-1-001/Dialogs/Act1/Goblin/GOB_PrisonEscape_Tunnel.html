    Synopsis:

    A body is found protruding out of a tunnel in the GOB prison. For patch 6: After pulling the body out, the player can use the tunnel to instantly escape. Patch 7: the player will need to find a shovel to dig their way out, but this will be outside the dialogue.

    How to trigger:

    Go to GOB prison and click on either the tunnel or the body that is sticking out of it. Recon 'oe gotogobprison' to be teleported there
