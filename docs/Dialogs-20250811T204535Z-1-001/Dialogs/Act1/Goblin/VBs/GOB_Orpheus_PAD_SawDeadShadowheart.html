    Synopsis:

    The party comments on seeing Shadowheart dead somehow (she was killed by the Orpheus artefact, which then teleported to the player's campsite). They remark with surprise on seeing her. If they subsequently use Speak with Dead, they receive a hint as to what happened to her.

    How to trigger:

    * Don't recruit Shadowheart
    * Start Protection from the Voice of the Absolute event by entering Goblin Camp (or try to leave to Act2/Act1B
    * When Shadowheart appears, don't recruit her and either:
    A) Left to Act2/Act1B
    B) Start a fight with her in the dialog, run away without killing her and take a Long Rest.
    * Return to the place you've seen her and approach her dead body.
