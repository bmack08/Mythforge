    Synopsis:

    A trader from the Zhentarim (z-HENT-ah-rim) , a shady organisation of spies, assassins and dealers in illicit goods. The player may or may not recognise her as such, but either way her attitude is blunt, breezy and unscrupulous. She's there to make money, and you're either there to make a deal or wasting her time - she has absolutely no interest in indecision or moralizing.
