    Synopsis:

    Player finds a group of goblin kids throwing stones at a bear, spurred on by their mother. The bear is the arch druid Halsin, in bear form. He won't make this apparent to the player, even if they have Speak with Animals.

    How to trigger:

    Repro steps:
    1. Start the game
    2. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    3. goto 400 -37
    4. Approach the group before the Bear's cage (prison)
