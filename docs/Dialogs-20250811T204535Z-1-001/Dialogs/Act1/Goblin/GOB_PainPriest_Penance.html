    Synopsis:

    This occurs as Abdirak is beating the player. Breathless with excitement. Reveling in the act of penance - think preacher in 'god will save us, save us ALL!' mode as he, well, beats the crap out of the player. All of his reactions should be manic, joyous and vaguely sexual. Player is in front of him, facing a wall as he raises his weapon and beats the player. The weapon will be either a dagger, handaxe or a scourge.

    How to trigger:

    Talk to Abdirak, accept to do his penance and follow his instructions. This plays after he hit you.
    Make sure to have enough HP; this is not played at level 1 normally.
