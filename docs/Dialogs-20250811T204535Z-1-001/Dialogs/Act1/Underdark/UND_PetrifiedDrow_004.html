    Synopsis:

    This drow was petrified by a spectator.
    Now he is unpetrified.
    Worries about his companions and the group's leader - the wizard. He should seem in shock. Short sentences, short delivery.
    Leave after a long rest.

    How to trigger:

    Repro steps:
    1. Start the game.
    2. peace
    3. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    4. goto 131.852; 49.695; -235.948
    5. create OBJ_Oil_Of_The_Basilisk
    6. Apply it on the petrified drow
    7. Ctrl Shift K the Spectator (if he's attacking you)
    8. Speak to the Drow

    1. One variation is use oe und_pd_freedrow. Then talk to Tecothy (S_UND_PetrifiedDrow_004).
    2. Second variation is use oe und_pd_freedrow. Then ctrl+shift+K on Dhourn (S_UND_PetrifiedDrow_Wizard) to kill him before talking to Tecothy.
    3. Third variation is use goto 121; -235, then use god on, addDebugSpell Target_UND_Spectator_UnpetrifyingRay, and infiniteAp. Climb up the rocks to the north. A Spectator will appear and you will enter battle. Use the unpetrifying ray spell on Tecothy. Then Ctrl+Shift+K on Dhourn, Spectator, and Hook Horror. Then talk to Tecothy.
    4. There is a fourth variation where Dhourn is still petrified after the battle. I could not figure this out.
