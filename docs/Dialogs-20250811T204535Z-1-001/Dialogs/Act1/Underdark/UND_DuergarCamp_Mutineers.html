    Synopsis:

    Two duergar consider how their fellow duergar have fallen for this new cult that showed up. You can inspire in them a mutiny. If you do, they'll join you when you free Nere (UND_TheDrowNere_Mutiny).

    Brithvar is a Mind Master, a duergar with psionic skills. He wears a strange mask.

    How to trigger:

    Go to UND Grymforge

    Repro steps:
    1. Start the game.
    2. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    3. goto -644 361
    4. go up the stair and talk to Elder Brithvar
