    Synopsis:

    The gnome leader's main dialogue. He thanks you for saving them, and tells you what brought his people to the Underdark to begin with - an expedition led by their charismatic leader Wulbren, to find a forgotten gnomish explosive of awesome power - runepowder.

    How to trigger:

    Resolve the cave-in situation in Grymforge by removing the rubble. A conflict ensues - this dialogue picks up on the various outcomes of that conflict.
    Repro steps:
    1. Start the game.
    2. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    3. goto -635.141; 14.738; 310
    4. CTRL-Shift_K on the cave-in
    5. Resolve the dialog by killing NERE and the Duerqars
    6. Speak with Beldron
