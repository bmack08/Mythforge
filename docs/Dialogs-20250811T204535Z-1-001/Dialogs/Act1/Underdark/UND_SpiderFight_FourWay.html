    Synopsis:

    In this dialogue, Lur Xanta is trying to convince the other 3 to leave with her to join the cult of Lolth.
    Player can help her convince them, or fail to do so and they will attack Lur Xanta for being disloyal.

    This dialogue has 2 outcomes:

    AGGRO: Lur Jox (or another spider) attacks Lur Xanta, killing her in one shot. (via gameplay - not cine)
    PEACEFUL: Lur Xanta convinces the other 3 to follow her into the Underdark-- they are leaving to somewhere new.

    Lur Xanta is fantatical and somewhat learned. The other 3 are a bit more simple.

    How to trigger:

    Repro steps:
    1. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    2. goto -621.967; 1.504; 362.3
    3. Talk to any of the spiders.
