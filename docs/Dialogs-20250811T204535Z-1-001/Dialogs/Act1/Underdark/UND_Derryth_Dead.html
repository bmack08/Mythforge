    Synopsis:

    Speak with Dead dialogue with Derryth Bonecloak, a dwarven alchemist from Baldur's Gate who came to the Underdark along with her husband, Baelen, in search of a rare and valuable mushroom - the noblestalk.

    How to trigger:

    1. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    2. goto 55.273; 42.457; -93.8
    3. CTRL-Shift-K on the NPC to kill it
    4. create OBJ_Scroll_SpeakWithDead 10
    5. Use the scroll on the corpse.

    The player is the killer -
    1. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    2. goto 55.273; 42.457; -93.8
    3. Attack the npc and kill it
    4. create OBJ_Scroll_SpeakWithDead 10
    5. Use the scroll on the corpse..
