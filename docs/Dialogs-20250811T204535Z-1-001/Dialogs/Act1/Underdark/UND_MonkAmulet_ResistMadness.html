    Synopsis:

    Player has left the camp. A few moments later, they are overcome with an unnatural sense of cheer. If they fail the check, they will fall over in laughter every time they use the amulet's special power that day.

    The madness has several levels.

    How to trigger:

    Leave camp or equip the amulet after using successfully using the amulet's unique ability multiple times during the previous day

    Repro steps to get the amulet:
    1. Start the game
    2. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    3. goto -626 224
    4. Unlock the Adamantine chest
    5. Take the amulet.

    Repro steps:
    1. Get the amulet
    2. Use the ability multiple times.
    3. Go to Camp.
    4. Fully rest and leave camp.
    5. Equip or stow amulet.
