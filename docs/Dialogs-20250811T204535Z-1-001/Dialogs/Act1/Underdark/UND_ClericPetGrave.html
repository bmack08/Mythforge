    Tombstone on the grave at Sussur Tree. Digging the grave unveils a collar of a pet (cat or dog). The pet was of cleric of Mystra who lived in the Arcane Tower nearby. This collar has no use on the first sight, at least there's no magical properties. But the secret is it allowed cleric's pet to have food or navigate inside the tower.
