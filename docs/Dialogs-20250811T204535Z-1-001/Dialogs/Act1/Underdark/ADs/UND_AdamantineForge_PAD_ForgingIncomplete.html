    Adamantine forge: players have inserted mould into the mould chamber, mithral into the forge, clicked the lever to trigger the hammer and the platform went down.     Go to adamantine forge
    create UNI_LOOT_AdamantineForge_ScimitarMold
    create UNI_PUZ_MithralOre
    fill mold chamber and forge
    click lever to trigger hammer strike
    click on the chamber or forge
