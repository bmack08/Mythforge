    Synopsis:

    Duergar dialog that happens if the player attacks / destroys the wall by fearful rothe.

    How to trigger:

    Repro steps:
    First use Debug Book: Go to region > Act 1 > UND - Underdark > Fearful Rothe
    Several variations in dialogue depending on what player does from here:
    Variation 1: Use roll amazing. Walk towards the nearby Rothe to trigger a conversation with Skarjall. Pick 1 > 2. Then talk to the Rothe. Pick 2 and roll the dice.
    Variation 2: Use roll amazing. Walk towards the nearby Rothe to trigger a conversation with Skarjall. Pick 1 > 1 > 1, roll dice, then Pick 1. Next talk to the Rothe. Pick 2 and roll dice.
    Variation 3: Walk towards the nearby Rothe to trigger a conversation with Skarjall. Pick Leave. Then Ctrl+Shift+K on the Cave-In (the rubble behind the Rothe).
