    Synopsis:

    Animal dialogue - a skittish beast of burden.A deep rothe that's being used by some duergar to clear rubble - only it smells death and is unwilling to work. The player can try to calm the beast (through Speak with Animals or Animal Handling) and encourage it to calm down and return to work, or to turn against the duergar. If the player fails to calm the rothe, it will turn on both them and the duergar.
