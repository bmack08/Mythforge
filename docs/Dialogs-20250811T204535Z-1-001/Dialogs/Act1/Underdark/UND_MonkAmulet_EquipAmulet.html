    Synopsis:

    When the player picks up the Monk's Amulet the monk's astral self appears and will start talking with the player. The player can find out who the monk is, what the amulet does. Later, they find that wearing the amulet will slowly drive them mad as they wear it (the monk has no control over this).

    How to trigger:

    Pick up or equip S_UND_MonkAmulet_Amulet_de2ea5a9-943a-4794-9ef7-208be7747178

    To trigger GREETING
    1. Enter the game as any character and 'loadlevel WLD_Main_A' into recon.
    2. goto -626.25, -4.53, 223.75.
    3. create OBJ_Scroll_Knock 10 and cast knock on the locked chest.
    4. Equip the amulet
