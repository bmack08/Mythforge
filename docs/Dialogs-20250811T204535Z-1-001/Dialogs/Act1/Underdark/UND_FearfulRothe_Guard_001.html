    Synopsis:

    The taskmaster is another cruel duergar. He is whipping several pack animals to clear out some rubble, but his arm is tired and asks you to step in. You might discover they want the rubble cleared because a valuable forge can be found behind it. If you convince the beasts to break through, he attacks you because he doesn't want to share the wealth.

    How to trigger:

    Speak to Herdmaster Skarjall - S_UND_FearfulRothe_Guard_001 (Position -668 13 442)
    Repro steps:
    1. Start the game
    2. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    3. goto -657, 443
    4. Walk towards the Rothe area
