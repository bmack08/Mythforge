    Synopsis:

    After a party member opens the valve that pours lava out of on the forging platform, a golem climbs out of the pipes, attacking them.

    Note: The valve can be activated by (ranged) attacks and spells, so party member positioning is not guaranteed.

    Note 2: Party members who are not players can activate the valve.

    How to trigger:

    Repro steps:
    1. Start the game.
    2. loadlevel WLD_Main_A
    3. goto -557.349; -3.987; 234
    4. oe UND_AdamantineForge_Down
    5. Turn the lava valve
