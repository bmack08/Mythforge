    Synopsis:

    The player attempts to remove their tadpole with the device, not realising that it will kill them as it does so. The player has opportunity to save themselves from the machine. If they fail or refuse those opportunities, Daisy will intervene. Variations based on whether the doctor and Lae'zel are present or not.

    How to trigger:

    start game with new character, add companions using oe debugbook
    loadlevel CRE_Main_A
    teleport S_CRE_Doctor
    as a companion, speak w doctor
    say your friend needs a tadpole removed
    switch to plyr and speak with doctor
    accept help and interact with zaith'isk
