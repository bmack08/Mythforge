    Synopsis:

    This is an all-purpose combat-starting AD on the Inquisitor, if and when he turns the full crèche hostile. It plays for a number of edge-cases that are the result of the player trespassing and if the player leaves the security office during the Vlaakith scene. It also plays at the start of the combat when he betrays the player on Vlaakith's command.

    How to trigger:

    Go to 1357; 0; -762 without watchin the captain-templar dialog in the captain's office. It will trigger the tresspassing reaction from the templar and start the attack.
