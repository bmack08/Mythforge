    Synopsis:

    The inquisitor demands the Orpheus artefact. The player can ask questions or attempt to hand over the artefact, which leads to Vlaakith appearing through the planecaster. Vlaakith explains that there's a prisoner inside who needs to be killed for the safety of the gith and teleports the player into the Astral Prison. Alternatively the player can kill the inquisitor and prompt Vlaakithâ€™s appearance themselves.

    How to trigger:

    Enter the security office room in the lower gith creche or get spotted while sneaking into it. Detect thoughts is a useful spell to have for this dialogue.
