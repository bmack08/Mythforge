    Synopsis:

    The player sees two Githyanki guards dragging three cultists in the monastery. One of the cultists - upon realising they're looking for some sort of weapon and failing to plead ignorance - makes a break for it. The gith guard kills him. The guards order the two remaining inside, pointing at the gate, before cutting to gameplay.

    How to trigger:

    Approach the main courtyard entrance of the monastery.
