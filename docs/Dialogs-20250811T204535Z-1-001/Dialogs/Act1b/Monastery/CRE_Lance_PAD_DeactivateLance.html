    Synopsis:

    Player has disarmed the lance of the final dawn that was about to destroy the building, deactivating the weapon. Player is glad it is over.

    How to trigger:

    - Go to CRE_Main_A
    - In ReCon, enter "oe cre_testlance"
    - Take the Blood of Lathander
    - Interact with the pedestal, and insert the Dawnmaster's crest OR Shoot down the four targets around the platform. When all 4 are destroyed the lance is disarmed.
    - Start walking back up the long corridor.
