    Group Discussion converted to Voicebark
    This is played after the player escapes the destruction of the Gith Creche.
    This intentionally excludes Laezel, who has an origin moment if she is present instead.

    How to trigger:

    Load up CRE_Main_A
    open recon
    use "oe cre_testlance"
    take the blood of lathander
    teleport party to -12; 18; -13
    exit turn based mode and wait for the destruction cinematic to play
    the voicebark will play after it ends
