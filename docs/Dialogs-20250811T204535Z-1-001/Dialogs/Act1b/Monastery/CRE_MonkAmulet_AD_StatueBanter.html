    Synopsis:

    The spirit inside the Sentient Amulet, once a monk of Lathander, sees a statue of Lathander in the interior courtyard of the Gith Creche, once a Lathander monastery. Pilgrims of old used to visit the statue to see a holy relic - the Blood of Lathander - though unbeknownst to them, it was a fake prop. The Mad Monk reminisces about his own pilgrimage.

    - Use ReCon command "oe MonkAmulet_Get" and equip the amulet.
    - Approach the statue of Lathander in the Creche: S_CRE_MonkAmulet_StatueBanter_f83fe070-3bae-4996-a349-7e14f0a0573a
