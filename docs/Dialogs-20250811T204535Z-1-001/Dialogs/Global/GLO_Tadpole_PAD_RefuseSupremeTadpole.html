    Companion characters can't use the supreme tadpole, as only the avatar can choose to transform into a Mind Flayer. They respond in rejection to using the tadpole when the player trys to make them use it.

    The exception being Karlach, who can transform but needs the avatar to make that decision.

    How to trigger:

    oe end_supremetadpole
    give supreme tadpole to non-avatar character
    Use the supreme tadpole item
