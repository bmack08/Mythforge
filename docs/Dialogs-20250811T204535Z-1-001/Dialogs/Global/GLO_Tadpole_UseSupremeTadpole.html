    Synopsis:

    The player made the choice to become a Mind Flayer, but held off on transforming right away, so the Emperor gave them a supreme tadpole to use when they decided to make the switch.

    Using the supreme tadpole item will trigger this dialog, which can happen in and out of combat.

    How to trigger:

    oe end_supremetadpole
    use the supreme tadpole

    Three different context:
    Out of Combat
    In Combat
    On the Netherbrain (oe brainbattle)
