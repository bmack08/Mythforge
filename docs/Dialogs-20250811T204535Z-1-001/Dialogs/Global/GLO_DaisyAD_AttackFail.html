    Synopsis:

    After the first Daisy Dream/Daisy Intro, Daisy may react to a successful attack for a few times.

    How to trigger:

    - Load WLD_Main_A
    - Queue the DaisyDream1 camp night
    - After the camp night, perform a failed attack that should do damage.
    - Make sure neither you nor Daisy are in a dialogue.
    - This will play three times per user and then never again.
