    Synopsis:

    The player dropped Ketheric's stone before leaving Colony or before moving to Intermezzo without picking it up again. The stone is needed to finish the game, leaving it there is bad. The player would have needed to of picked up the stone then dropped it before attempting to leave.

    How to trigger:

    Use debug book to go to Ketheric Showdown in Colony
    Proceed and kill Ketheric
    Take his stone
    Drop the stone
    Attempt to leave
    or take the stone through the portal
    drop the stone
    attempt to travers through the war camp
