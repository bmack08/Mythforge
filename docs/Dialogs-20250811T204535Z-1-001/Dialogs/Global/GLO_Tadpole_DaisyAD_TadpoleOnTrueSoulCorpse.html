    Synopsis:

    Daisy speaks as a voice in our heads on approaching the dead body of a True Soul.

    How to trigger:

    Kill a character with an ILLITHID tag (corpse will contain a tadpole). If no one else is speaking and the player can see the tadpole from the corpse, Daisy will say this AD.
