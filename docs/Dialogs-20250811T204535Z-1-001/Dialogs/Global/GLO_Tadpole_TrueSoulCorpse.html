    Synopsis:

    The player witnesses an invasive parasite emerge from a dead body - the very same kind of parasite that currently resides inside them. They try to kill it, but find this curiously difficult - the little creature can psychically influence the player and tries to sway them from doing it harm. Meanwhile, Daisy encourages us to harvest it to absorb its potential.

    How to trigger:

    Loot the tadpole from a True Soul corpse for the first time.
